# Search 01 — Research (Chewy CHWY)

Goal: Identify Chewy's most recent quarter and the load-bearing figures (revenue, growth, active customers, guidance).

Queries:
- "Chewy CHWY Q1 fiscal 2025 earnings results net sales growth active customers May 2026"
- "Chewy CHWY latest quarterly earnings 2026 net sales active customers guidance"

Findings:
- Most recent quarter = Q1 fiscal 2026, thirteen weeks ended May 3, 2026, reported June 10, 2026 (not Q1 FY2025, which ended May 4, 2025).
- Net sales $3.36B, +7.7% YoY. Prior-year comparable (Q1 FY2025) was +8.3% — confirms deceleration thesis.
- Active customers 21.5M, +3.6% YoY from 20.8M; ~200K sequential net adds.
- Full-year FY2026 net sales guidance cut to $13.40–$13.55B (6.3%–7.5% growth).
- Customer-add guidance softened to low end of ~150K–250K/quarter "given the current environment."
- Adjusted EPS $0.43, beat consensus ~$0.24; stock fell ~4% premarket on the guidance.

Thesis verified as accurate: growth decelerating, customers roughly flat, guidance cut. Proceeded to draft.
