# Sources — Guardant Health (GH), diagnostics

Article: `2026-06-17T1130-guardant.md`
Report date: Q1 2026 results reported May 7–8, 2026.

## Primary facts (confirmed)

- Q1 2026 revenue $302M, +48% YoY (prior-year quarter ~$203.5M); fastest growth in 5 years; trailing-12-month revenue crossed $1B.
- Oncology revenue $205M, +36%, ~86,000 tests, +47% volume.
- Biopharma & Data revenue $53M, +17%.
- Screening revenue $41.6M (vs $5.7M prior-year period, >600% growth); Shield volume ~44,000 tests vs ~9,000.
- Net loss $112.1M (widened from $95.2M a year earlier); EPS -$0.85.
- Q1 2026 free cash flow burn $71M (YoY rise mostly higher bonus payout; ex-bonus down $12M).
- FY2025 FCF burn $233M, improved from $275M in FY2024.
- FY2026 guidance raised to $1.30B–$1.32B (~32%–34% growth); screening guidance $162M–$174M from 210k–225k Shield tests.
- Late-2025 capital: $327.3M public offering + $402.5M convertible senior notes.
- Shield Medicare reimbursement $1,495/test; screening gross margin 56% (from 18%) on Shield cost-per-test to $420 + higher ASP.
- Shield Eclipse study: CRC sensitivity 83.1%; advanced-adenoma sensitivity 13.2%; specificity ~90%.
- Exact Sciences Cologuard Plus: CRC sensitivity ~93.9%; advanced-adenoma sensitivity ~43.4%.
- Abbott developing a competing blood-based colorectal screen.
