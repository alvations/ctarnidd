# Search 01 — outage + valuation + competition (read 2026-06-11)

Queries: "CrowdStrike CRWD 2026 stock valuation market cap revenue ARR P/E forward all-time high recovery after
July 2024 global outage"; "CrowdStrike July 2024 outage 8.5 million Windows devices Delta lawsuit cost ...
cybersecurity competition Microsoft".

- July 19 2024: flawed Falcon Sensor update crashed ~8.5M Windows systems globally (aviation, banking,
  healthcare, media); bad file loaded at boot → boot loops requiring manual remediation; largest IT outage in
  history.
- Delta: canceled 5,000 (suit says 7,000+) flights, manually reset 40,000 servers, 1.3M passengers; claims
  ~$500M cost; Delta sued CRWD, CRWD countersued; shareholder suit dismissed, Delta case continues (no trial
  date at time of search-1).
- Valuation (search-1, partly STALE — see search-03): ~$419 ATH / +115% / $102.7B cap; ~95x forward earnings,
  ~30-31x sales (P/S 31.45). FY2026 rev guide $4.74-4.8B; growth ~21.4%, EPS growth ~16.7%.
- Competition: Microsoft Defender bundled with E5 licenses (wins on price/ubiquity, "biggest obstacle to
  pricing power"); Palo Alto bundling aggressively; CRWD best-in-breed esp non-Windows/complex detection.
- "Premium valuation leaves very little margin for error."
