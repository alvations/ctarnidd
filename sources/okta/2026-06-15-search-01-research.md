## Findings

- Okta reported Q1 FY2027 on May 28, 2026 (quarter ended April 2026).
- Total revenue $765M, +11% YoY; subscription revenue also +11%.
- RPO +16% YoY (total RPO $4.084B); current RPO +12% YoY to $2.499B.
- Non-GAAP diluted EPS $0.91, beat $0.85 estimate.
- Operating cash flow $277M; free cash flow $271M.
- FY2026: revenue $2.919B (+12%); cRPO $2.513B (+12%); non-GAAP operating income $766M (26%).
- FY2027 guidance: revenue $3.185B–$3.205B (9–10%); non-GAAP op margin 25–26%; FCF $855M–$885M.
- Q2 FY27 guidance: revenue $790M–$794M (9%), cRPO $2.505B–$2.515B, non-GAAP op income $204M–$208M.
- ~1pt revenue headwind from accelerating professional-services shift to partners.

## Thesis verification

Premise CONFIRMED and strengthened: growth has decelerated from 50%+ hypergrowth peak to 11%, with FY27 guidance of 9–10% — slowest in public history. Entra bundling and new-product pivot (governance, privileged access) are live themes. Thesis holds; no pivot needed.
