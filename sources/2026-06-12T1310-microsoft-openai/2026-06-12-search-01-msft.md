Q3 FY2026 (qtr ended Mar 31 2026):
- Total revenue **$82.9B** (+18%; vs ~$81.4-81.5B consensus). **Azure +39-40%** constant currency. AI business
  ~**$37B run rate**.
- **Gross margin 67.6%** — narrowest since 2022 (data-center depreciation). (MSFT Cloud gross margin 66%.)
- FY2026 capex ~**$190B** (+~61% vs ~$80B FY25), incl ~**$25B** from higher memory/component prices.

OPENAI:
- MSFT holds ~**27%** of OpenAI for-profit (equity method); stake valued ~**$135B**. Total funding commitment
  **$13B**, **$11.8B funded** by Mar 31 2026.
- OpenAI committed **$250B** incremental Azure spend (Oct 2025 recap). API rights through 2030; IP through 2032;
  OpenAI's 20% rev-share to MSFT through 2030 (now capped); Azure exclusivity REMOVED; AGI-termination clause
  removed; MSFT no longer controlling investor.
- Equity-method whiplash: ~$3.1B income DROP from OpenAI losses (qtr ~Oct 2025) → **$5.9B net GAINS** for 9mo to
  Mar 31 2026 — driven by a **dilution gain** when OpenAI restructured into a PBC (Oct 2025): smaller % but
  implied valuation rose faster → booked the difference as income. Non-cash; OpenAI still deeply unprofitable.
- Circular: MSFT cash → OpenAI → Azure compute → MSFT revenue.
