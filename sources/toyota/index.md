## Primary facts (all CONFIRMED)

- Net revenue FY2026: 50.684 trillion yen, +5.5% YoY (record).
- Operating income FY2026: 3.8 trillion yen, down ~21.5% from ~4.795 trillion yen prior year.
- Net income attributable to Toyota: 3.848 trillion yen vs 4.765 trillion prior (~−19.2%); ≈ $25.7B vs ≈ $31.7B.
- North America: operating loss ≈ 298.6 billion yen ≈ $1.9 billion; first regional loss in 16 years (prior 2008–09). Regional margin ≈ −1.4%. NA sales +8.5% to 2.934 million units.
- U.S. tariff impact on consolidated operating income: 1.38 trillion yen (1,380.0 billion yen).
- Unfavorable FX reduced earnings by ~$13.5 billion.
- FY2027 guidance: operating income 3.0 trillion yen (down ~766.2 billion yen / ~20%); FX assumption 150 yen/USD; BEV sales projected to more than double to 598,000 units.
- TM ADR trading near $188 at time of FY2026 coverage.
- Prior context: FY2026 Q1 (Apr–Jun 2025) operating income 1.166 trillion yen (down from 1.308T); Q1 tariff impact 450 billion yen.
