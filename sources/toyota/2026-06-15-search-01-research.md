# Search 01 — research (2026-06-15)

Queries:
- "Toyota TM earnings fiscal year 2025 full year results operating profit yen"
- "Toyota fiscal 2026 first quarter results April-June 2025 operating income tariff impact"

Purpose: establish the most recent reported period and the revenue/operating-income trajectory; confirm tariffs as the dominant margin pressure.

Findings:
- FY2025 (ended Mar 2025): net revenue 48.036 trillion yen (+6.5%); operating income fell from 5.352T to 4.795T yen; net income 4.765T yen.
- FY2026 Q1 (Apr–Jun 2025, reported Aug 7, 2025): operating income 1.166T yen (down from 1.308T); net revenue 12.253T yen (+3.5%); consolidated vehicle sales ~2,411,000 (+~159,000). U.S. tariff impact ~450 billion yen in the quarter.
- Initial FY2026 forecast assumed 145 yen/USD.

Conclusion: trajectory is rising revenue / falling operating profit, with tariffs the explicit driver — thesis premise (margin compression masked by record revenue) supported. Proceeded to confirm full-year FY2026 actuals.
