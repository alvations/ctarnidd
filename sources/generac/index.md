# Sources — Generac (GNRC)

## Primary facts (Q1 2026, reported April 29, 2026)

- Consolidated net sales: $1.06B, +12% YoY (vs $942M).
- Residential external net sales: ~$549M, +~1% (vs ~$543M). Home standby approximately flat (price offset lower volume); energy storage declined; winter storm Fern aided home standby.
- Prior-year strength partly from "substantial 2024 hurricane season."
- C&I external net sales: ~$510M, +~28% (vs $399M). Data-center-driven.
- Residential adj. EBITDA margin 25.1% ($138.6M); C&I adj. EBITDA margin 13.0%.
- GAAP net income $73M ($1.24/sh); adjusted net income $106M ($1.80/sh); adj. EBITDA ~$193.5M (18.3%); gross margin 38.7%.
- Free cash flow ~$90M (vs $27.2M); cash from operations $119M.
- Adjusted EPS $1.80 beat consensus ~$1.33.
- FY2026 guidance raised: net sales mid-to-high teens, adj. EBITDA margin 18.5–19.5%, C&I mid-to-high 20s. Raise "driven entirely by" data center/telecom/rental in C&I.
- Data-center backlog over $700M; "final stages of vendor approval with multiple hyperscale customers."
- Stock ~+8.6% pre-market on print; ~+90% trailing year vs industry ~25%.
