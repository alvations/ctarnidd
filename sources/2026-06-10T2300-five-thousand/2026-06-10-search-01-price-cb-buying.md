# Search 01 — gold price + central-bank buying (read 2026-06-10/11)

Queries: "gold price 2026 above $5000 central bank buying record debasement de-dollarization all-time high";
"central banks gold buying 2025 2026 tonnes record reserves diversification away from dollar Treasuries".

- Gold broke $5,000/oz first time Mon Jan 26 2026 (~$5,110–5,115 intraday); ATH ~$5,589 Jan 28 2026; still
  >$5,000 as of Mar 17 2026. First time above 1980 peak in REAL (inflation-adjusted) terms — genuine new
  price discovery, not inflation catch-up.
- Central-bank buying: well above pre-2022 norm (~400–500 t/yr); 1,000+ tonnes/yr in 2022–2024 (third+
  consecutive record years). One source: record 1,237t in 2025; another: 863t 2025 (conflict — see search-02).
- 43% of central banks plan to increase gold reserves in 2026 (up from 29% two years ago).
- Driver: post-2022 Russia $300B FX-reserve freeze taught CBs dollar reserves can be weaponized; gold can't
  be frozen/sanctioned/devalued — no one's liability.
- Morgan Stanley: gold now a larger share of CB reserves than US Treasuries — first time since 1996.
- Forecasts: JPM ~755t CB purchases 2026, gold pushing $6,000 by end-2026, $6,300 possible 2027; BofA $6,000
  by spring 2026.
