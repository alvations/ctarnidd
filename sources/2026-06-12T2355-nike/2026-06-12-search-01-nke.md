# Search 01 — research (Nike)

Query: Nike NKE Q3 fiscal 2026 earnings revenue decline China gross margin inventory turnaround guidance.

- Gross margin 40.2%, −130bps (tariffs in NA + promotions).

- Turnaround ("Win Now"): inventory cleanup + product innovation, "taking longer than anticipated."

## Forensic angle selected
Turnaround-not-rebound: market prices the snap-back while revenue/margins/China all still point down and mgmt says
recovery is slower than hoped. Plus self-inflicted DTC/innovation wounds (slow to undo), China structural decline,
two-sided margin squeeze, competitors (On/Hoka/NB/Adidas) holding ground, inventory not yet cleared.
