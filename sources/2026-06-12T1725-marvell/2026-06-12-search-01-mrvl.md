Q1 FY2027:
- Record revenue **$2.418B** (+28% YoY; beat guide). Q2 FY27 guide ~$2.7B, adj EPS $0.93. FY27 ~30-40% growth.
- **Data center ~$1.83B = ~76% of total** (more than doubled YoY; "on fire"). [Search-01 also cited an $816M /
  +109% figure → VERIFY shows that was the year-AGO / prior-basis number; current DC = $1.83B. Corrected.]
- **Custom silicon ~$1.5B annual run rate**; custom-ASIC market forecast **+45%** 2026.
- Customers: **Alphabet, Amazon, Microsoft**. **Nvidia $2B investment / expanded partnership** (Mar 31 2026;
  custom silicon + optical into Nvidia ecosystem).

VALUATION / COMPETITION:
- **Broadcom = leader, >70%** custom-AI-accelerator share (customers Google, Meta, OpenAI, Anthropic, Apple).
  **Marvell targeting ~20%.** Marvell trailing P/E ~28, PEG ~1; **Broadcom now trades at a LOWER forward P/E than
  Marvell** (Marvell = premium to the leader).
- Risk: customer **concentration** (few hyperscaler programs; slowdown/in-source/delay hits fast); customers
  building in-house chip teams; competes w/ Nvidia/AMD merchant GPUs; **TSMC** shared chokepoint.
- Marvell also strong in **optical interconnect** (more diversified, higher-quality, under-emphasized).
