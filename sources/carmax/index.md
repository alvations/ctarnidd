## Primary facts (Q1 FY2027, all from CarMax IR / 8-K, cross-checked)

- Net revenues: $8.0 billion, +6.2% YoY.
- Diluted EPS: $1.31 (vs ~$0.94 Zacks consensus; vs $1.38 prior-year quarter).
- Net earnings: $185.6 million, down 11.8% from $210.4 million.
- Combined retail + wholesale unit sales: 392,357, +3.3%.
- Comparable-store used unit sales: -0.8% YoY.
- Gross profit per retail used unit: $2,177, down $230 from prior-year all-time record.
- Gross profit per wholesale unit: $1,046 (~flat).
- SG&A: $635.2 million, down 3.7% / $24.5 million; SG&A per total unit $1,619, improved $118 (6.8%).
- CAF income: $140.2 million, down 1.0%; attributed to lower loans outstanding after $900M non-prime securitization (Q3 prior FY).
- CAF financed 43.3% of units (after 3-day payoffs), up from 41.8%.
- Provision for loan losses: $95.6 million vs $101.7 million prior year, including a $25.1 million one-time allowance release on loans reclassified held-for-sale.
- Cross-check, Q4 FY2026: provision $73.9M vs $68.3M, tied to higher Tier 2 penetration.
