# Sources — Deckers (DECK), footwear

Article: 2026-06-17T1300-deckers.md
Report date for the load-bearing quarter: Q4 & Full FY2026 results released May 21, 2026.

## Primary facts (confirmed)

- FY2026 net sales: $5.47B, +10% YoY (~9.8%).
- Hoka FY2026: $2.59B, +16% (~15.9%). Q4 Hoka: $671M, +15%, largest quarter ever.
- Ugg FY2026: $2.74B, +8% (~8.2%).
- Hoka + Ugg ≈ 97% of total revenue.
- Q4 FY2026 revenue: $1.12B, +10%, beat ~$1.09B consensus.
- FY2026 diluted EPS: $7.02 vs $6.33, +11%. Operating margin above 23%.
- FY2027 guidance: revenue $5.86–5.91B; diluted EPS $7.30–7.45.
- FY2027 gross margin guide ~56.5%, down YoY (freight / Middle East shipping disruption, input/material costs).
- Tariffs: $120M paid; assumes 10% rate full year, no refund (refund pursuit ongoing).
- Through-2030 framework: high-single-digit revenue CAGR; Hoka low double digits; Ugg mid-single digits; EPS low double digits.
- June quarter: Hoka guided high-single-digit growth on "one-time wholesale timing shifts."
- Stock ~$110.17 (June 16, 2026); ~14.9x forward P/E; Raymond James cut Strong Buy → Outperform on valuation.
- $5B buyback authorization.
