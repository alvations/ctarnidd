# Search 01 — research (Airbnb)

Query: Airbnb Q1 2026 earnings revenue nights booked growth deceleration guidance experiences reinvestment FCF.

## Findings used
- Revenue +18% to $2.7B (beat); GBV +19%; nights & seats booked +9% to 156.2M (+13.1M YoY).
- FCF $1.704B; FY adj EBITDA margin guide ≥35%.
- Localized strategy (Brazil, India, Japan) drove first-time users; Experiences relaunch + Services push.
- FY2026 guidance raised; Q2 nights growth expected to slightly decelerate (~100bps Middle East headwind).

## Forensic angle selected
The maturation gap: nights +9% vs revenue +18% = the core volume slowing to single digits while price + take-rate
carry the headline. Plus fee/affordability ceiling, STR regulation in the richest cities, Experiences/Services as a
second-act reinvestment (failed once before), Booking as the broader rival. Concede the raised guidance.
