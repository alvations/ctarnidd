# Search 01 — research (Coca-Cola)

Query: Coca-Cola KO Q1 2026 earnings organic revenue growth unit case volume price mix GLP-1 guidance.

- GLP-1: KO research says users shift to low/no-cal (Coke Zero, Diet Coke, smartwater, Fairlife, sports), smaller
  packs; launched single-serve mini cans (Jan 2026).
- K-shaped economy: premium + value split strategy.

## Forensic angle selected

+10% vs FY guide +4-5%. Plus affordability ceiling (K-shaped), GLP-1 structural volume threat, defensive bond-proxy
premium. Volume = compounding engine; price = finite ratchet.
