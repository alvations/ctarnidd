## Content recovered
- **Mechanism:** AP creates new ETF shares (inflow), then within a few days redeems an equal amount **in
  kind**, letting the ETF offload **appreciated/low-basis securities** via the redemption basket WITHOUT a
  taxable event. Deployed around **rebalancing dates** when appreciated stocks leave the index.
- **Legal basis: IRC §852(b)(6)** — if an ETF distributes appreciated securities in kind, **no gain
  recognized**; allows deferral and even **complete avoidance** unavailable to mutual funds / direct holders.
- **Custom basket exemption:** SEC **Rule 6c-11 (2019, "ETF Rule")** let ETFs deploy heartbeats more
  efficiently (redemption basket = only the appreciated securities leaving).
- Debate: "defer, not dodge" (etf.com) vs academics (avoidance, esp. via step-up at death).
