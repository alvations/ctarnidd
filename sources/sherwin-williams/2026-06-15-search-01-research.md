# Search 01 — Research (SHW Q1 2026 results)

Queries:
- "Sherwin-Williams Q1 2026 earnings results revenue sales guidance"
- "Sherwin-Williams Q1 2026 paint volume comparable sales Paint Stores Group margin"

Key findings:
- Q1 2026 reported April 28, 2026. Net sales $5.67B, +6.8% YoY; beat ~$5.56B estimate.
- Growth aided by Oct 2025 Suvinil acquisition + favorable FX translation.
- Gross margin 49.1%, +90 bps; 14th consecutive quarter of expansion.
- Adjusted diluted EPS $2.35 (beat ~$2.27).
- Paint Stores Group: +3.7% to $3.05B; same-store sales +2.4%.
- Consumer Brands: Suvinil high-teens growth; North American DIY demand declined organically.
- FY2026 reaffirmed: net sales up low-to-mid single digit %; GAAP EPS $10.70–$11.10; adjusted EPS $11.50–$11.90.
- Management: "little to no recovery in most end markets this year"; raw-material inflation (propylene), tariff/FX risk; 80–100 new stores planned.
