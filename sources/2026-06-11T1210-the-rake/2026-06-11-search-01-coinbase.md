Stablecoin economics:
- Circle issues USDC (reserves ~T-bills); Coinbase captures ~**50%** of USDC economics, incl USDC held off

- Threat: **Hyperliquid** USDC deal → Hyperliquid captures up to **90%** of reserve income on its USDC deposits;
  could shift ~**$160M** revenue from Coinbase/Circle to Hyperliquid. Precedent for others to demand a cut.
