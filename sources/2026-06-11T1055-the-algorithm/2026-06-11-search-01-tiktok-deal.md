# Search 01 — TikTok US deal structure (read 2026-06-11)

Queries: "TikTok divestiture deal 2026 ByteDance US joint venture valuation Oracle"; "ByteDance valuation 2026";
"TikTok US deal algorithm licensed not sold"; "Oracle RPO backlog 2026 TikTok hosting debt capex".

- Deal closed Jan 22, 2026: TikTok USDS Joint Venture LLC. 80.1% American-owned / ByteDance retains **19.9%**.
- Ownership: new investors (Oracle, Silver Lake, MGX) 15% each = 50%; existing ByteDance investors 30.1%;
  ByteDance 19.9%. ~170M US users.
- Valuation: TikTok US ~**$14B** (VP Vance cited). Oracle's 15% stake ~$2B (Mar 2026 filing).
- ByteDance parent: ~**$550B** private valuation (up from $400B); 2024 rev $155B, 2025 target ~$186B, ~$48B
  profit; now larger than Meta by revenue. Largest outside holder Susquehanna; also Gen Atlantic, KKR, SoftBank.
- Algorithm: **licensed** from ByteDance, "retrained solely on U.S. data" — not a full sale. House Select
  Committee on CCP: "It wouldn't be in compliance if the algorithm is Chinese... There can't be any shared
  algorithm with ByteDance." Foreign Policy: China "gets the best of both worlds." Critics: "the algorithm
  isn't going to change." Time: deal may retain Chinese algorithm. Critics also warn it swaps Chinese
  surveillance for US surveillance.
- Oracle: RPO **$523B** (+~438% YoY), anchored by OpenAI/Meta/Nvidia + Stargate ($500B). FY26 capex ~$50B;
  total debt >**$124B**. Q3 FY26 cloud rev +44% to $8.9B. Oracle = TikTok US "trusted technology provider" /
  data host.
