# Sources — Dollar General (DG)

Article: 2026-06-18T0830-dollar-general.md
Subject: Dollar General Corporation (NYSE: DG), discount retail.
Latest quarter at time of writing: Q1 fiscal 2026, reported 2026-06-02 (quarter ended ~May 2026).

## Primary facts

- Net sales: $10.79B, +3.4% YoY.
- Same-store sales: +2.0% (traffic +1.4%, average transaction +0.5%).
- Gross margin: 31.6%, +65 bps (higher markups, lower shrink and damages).
- Operating profit: $638.5M, +10.8% (from $576.1M).
- Operating margin: 5.9%, ~+40 bps via shrink mitigation / improved damages.
- Diluted EPS: $2.00, +12.4%. Net income: $444.1M, +13.3%.
- FY2026 guidance: EPS raised to $7.20–$7.45 (from $7.10–$7.35); net sales growth 3.7%–4.2%; SSS 2.2%–2.7%; capex $1.4B–$1.5B.
- FY2024 context: net sales +5.0% to $40.6B; SSS +1.4%; gross margin 29.6% (-70bps); SG&A 25.4% (+140bps).
- Year-ago quarter SG&A: 25.8% vs 24.6% (+121 bps), driven by labor, incentive comp, R&M, benefits.
- Tariffs: reduce China exposure, move sourcing, raise prices "as a last resort" (Vasos).
- Real-estate program: thousands of projects (new stores + Project Elevate / Project Renovate remodels); >20,000 stores.
