## Findings

- Q1 2026: net sales +6% to ~$1.14B; operating income +7% to $82.6M; margin 7.3%; EPS $1.45; gross margin 29.0% (-20 bps). Guidance $10.87-$11.17 confirmed. (Report date Apr 23, 2026.)
- FY2025: net sales ~$5.3B (flat vs 2024); net income ~$406.4M; EPS $10.85 (-4%); operating income -6%; S&A ~$992.3M (+4%).
- New pool builds: ~58,000-60,000 in 2025; 4th straight annual decline; ~40% below 2022 peak (~98,000). Permits fell again in 2025.
- Valuation: stock ~$195; forward P/E ~18x; 5-yr avg ~27x; down ~a third over 12 months.

## Pivot note

Suggested angle implied a revenue decline. Reality: total revenue FLAT for 3 years, not down; Q1 2026 grew 6%. Pivoted thesis to the multi-year plateau + structurally reset construction line + full multiple. Construction-unit decline (~40% off peak, 4 years) is accurate and load-bearing.
