# Search 01 — Hims & Hers GLP-1 (read 2026-06-12)

Queries: "Hims Hers HIMS 2026 Q1 revenue GLP-1 compounded semaglutide Novo lawsuit FDA shortage ended"; "Hims
2026 compounded GLP-1 revenue dependence FDA 503A 503B personalization loophole valuation subscribers".

Q1 2026:
- Revenue **$608.1M** (+4% from $586M) — big deceleration. Gross margin **65%** (from **73%**). Net loss
  **$92.1M** (~$0.41/sh) vs **+$49.5M** net income Q1 2025. **$33.5M** restructuring + **$15M** legal settlement.
- **2.6M** subscribers. FY2026 revenue guide raised to **$2.8-3.0B**. Most of 2025 revenue was **non-GLP-1**.

THE LOOPHOLE:
- FDA-declared shortage of semaglutide (Wegovy/Ozempic) & tirzepatide (Zepbound/Mounjaro) let 503A/503B
  compounding pharmacies make legal copies. Hims built a high-margin compounded-semaglutide DTC business.
- Shortage ENDED (Novo/Lilly ramped capacity) → compounding copies no longer permitted; FDA wind-down deadlines.
- "Personalization loophole": Hims argued formulations weren't "essentially copies" (added B vitamins). 503A can
  compound a personalized version only if a prescriber documents a clinically "significant difference" for that
  patient. FDA closed the door on GLP-1 API compounding (Feb 2026).

ENFORCEMENT / LEGAL:
- HHS General Counsel referred Hims to the **DOJ** for potential FD&C Act violations; Novo sued Hims (patent
  infringement). "End of the gray market."
- Pivot: Hims now resells **branded** Wegovy/Zepbound via Novo/Lilly collaborations to its subscribers. Peptide
  optionality (RFK Jr. peptide policy).
