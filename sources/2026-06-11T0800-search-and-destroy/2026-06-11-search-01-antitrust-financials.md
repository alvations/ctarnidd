# Search 01 — Alphabet financials + antitrust (read 2026-06-11)

Queries: "Alphabet GOOGL 2026 market cap revenue search advertising ... antitrust DOJ remedy Mehta no breakup
AI Overviews zero-click cannibalize"; plus earlier Google antitrust + AI search searches.

- Alphabet Q1 2026 rev +22% to $109.9B (11th straight double-digit qtr); Google Services +16% to $89.6B;
  Search & other +19%. Q4 2025 +18% to $113.8B. YouTube >$60B (FY2025).
- Antitrust (US v. Google search): Sept 2 2025 Judge Mehta remedies — illegal monopoly in search + search
  ads; NO breakup; banned exclusive default-search contracts; prohibited exclusive distribution of Search/
  Chrome/Assistant/Gemini; ordered sharing of search-index + user-interaction data with rivals (NOT ad data);
  Google keeps Chrome. GenAI threat cited as a reason a breakup was hard to justify.
- Appeals: Google filed Jan 16 2026 (challenging data-sharing + technical committee); DOJ cross-appeal Feb 3
  2026 (seeking stronger remedies incl. divestitures).
- AI search: zero-click ~60% (AI Mode/AI Overviews answer without clicks); UK CMA AI Overviews opt-out order
  (June 3 2026, enforceable June 17, excludes Gemini app). PriceRunner v. Google ~$8.3B (Stockholm, June 26).
- ~$180B capex AI bet.
