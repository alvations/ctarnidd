# Sources — Etsy (ETSY)

## Primary facts (Q1 2026, reported April 29, 2026)

- Total revenue: $631.3 million.
- Marketplace revenue growth: +7.6% YoY; continuing-operations revenue growth: +3.1%.
- Take rate: 25.7%, up 180 bps YoY; ~130 bps of the increase attributable to the Reverb divestiture; drivers Etsy Ads + marketplace fee changes.
- Etsy marketplace GMS: $2.46 billion, +5.5% YoY; currency-neutral +3.6%.
- Continuing-operations consolidated GMS: -3.9% YoY.
- Active buyers: first sequential growth in two years (exact Q1 2026 count not cleanly disclosed; ~86.6M TTM cited in one transcript summary).
- GMS per active buyer (TTM): $122, first YoY growth since 2022.
- Net income from continuing operations: $104.7 million (vs $(35.1)M loss Q1 2025).
- Adjusted EBITDA: $184.7 million; 29.3% margin.
- Mobile app GMS: 47% of total, +11.2% YoY.
- Depop sale to eBay: $1.2 billion; closing pushed to end of Q3 2026; U.S./Germany regulatory clearance received. (Depop acquired 2021 for ~$1.6B.)
- Reverb divested June 2025.
- Q2 2026 guidance: GMS $2.48–2.53B; adj EBITDA margin 27–29%; FY low-single-digit GMS growth.

## Buyer-decline history (context)

- Active buyers: 95.5M (end 2024) -> 88.5M (Q1 2025, -3.4%) -> 87.3M (Q2 2025, -4.6%) -> ~86.5M (Q4 2025, -3.4%).
- GMS per active buyer (TTM): ~$121 (Q4 2024) -> $120 (Q1/Q2 2025) -> $121 (Q4 2025) -> $122 (Q1 2026).

## Fee / competition context

- Etsy transaction fee raised 5% -> 6.5% in April 2022 (a 30% increase; prompted a seller strike).
- Standard seller fees ~10–11% of a typical US order (listing + transaction + payment processing).
- Competitors: Amazon Handmade (15% referral fee), Amazon marketplace + Prime, plus Temu / Shein / TikTok Shop on the price-sensitive flank.
