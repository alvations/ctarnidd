- Continuing-operations consolidated GMS -3.9% YoY; currency-neutral marketplace GMS +3.6%.

- Buyer-decline history: 95.5M (end 2024) -> 88.5M -> 87.3M -> ~86.5M through 2025.

Thesis pivot decision: original "buyers keep leaving / GMS keeps declining" premise has partially turned in Q1 2026 (GMS +5.5%, first sequential buyer growth). Pivoted to: the recovery is engineered via take-rate hikes + divestitures + FX, not organic buyer demand; denominator illusion in GMS/buyer; take-rate strategy is self-limiting vs Temu/Amazon.
