## Findings

- FY2026 results (year ended Feb 28, 2026) reported April 8, 2026.
- Q4 EPS $1.90 adjusted, beat ~$1.73 estimate; Q4 revenue $1.92B vs ~$1.84B est.
- FY profit $1.69B / $9.61 EPS; FY revenue ~$9.14B, down (beer-segment view -3%, consolidated -10%).
- Beer FY net sales -3%, shipments -3.8%, partially offset by favorable pricing/mix.
- Wine & Spirits FY net sales -58%, shipments -72.9% (divestitures + distributor-contract changes + strategic pricing).
- New CEO Nicholas Fink effective April 13, 2026.
- Beer depletions: latest quarter +0.6% but FY -2.1%; Modelo Especial ~-3%, Corona Extra ~-7%, Modelo Chelada ~-1%; Pacifico +15%, Victoria +16%.
- Canopy: $1.06B impairment was fiscal 2023 (historical); position reduced/exited.
- FY2026 outlook EPS guidance ~$11.30-$11.60.
- Beer operating-margin guidance cut to 37%-38% from 39%-40% (Veracruz fixed costs, SG&A, marketing) — CFO Garth Hankinson.
- FY beer revenue guidance -1% to +1%; "high volatility and low visibility."
- Q4 operating margin -340bps to 33.2% (fixed-cost absorption, depreciation, aluminum tariffs).
- Net leverage ~2.7x vs ~3.0x target; investment-grade; dividend raised 1% to $1.03; >$1.6B returned incl >$900M buybacks.
