# Search 01 — Research: SAP Q1 2026 results

Queries:
- "SAP Q1 2026 results cloud revenue backlog growth guidance"
- "SAP Q1 2026 earnings cloud backlog current cloud revenue operating profit margin"

Findings (SAP News Center, PR Newswire, SAPinsider, SEC 6-K; reported April 23, 2026):
- Total revenue €9.555B, +6% reported / +12% cc.
- Cloud revenue €5.962B, +19% reported / +27% cc.
- Cloud ERP Suite €5.214B, +23% reported / +30% cc.
- Current cloud backlog €21.9B, +20% reported / +25% cc.
- IFRS operating profit +17%; non-IFRS +17% reported / +24% cc; non-IFRS margin 30.0% (+2.9pp).
- FY2026 guidance maintained; cc cloud revenue growth ~23–25%; total revenue growth similar to 2025, accelerating in 2027.
- CFO Asam flagged a slight deceleration in current cloud backlog growth ahead; quarter-specific cloud tailwinds unlikely to re-occur.

Thesis crystallized: the wedge between the 27% cc cloud headline and the 6% reported total revenue — a currency-translation crutch plus migration-driven (RISE) re-papering of an existing base.
