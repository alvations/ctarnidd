# Sources — Cleveland-Cliffs (CLF)

## Primary facts (Q1 2026, period ended March 31, 2026; reported April 20, 2026)

- Consolidated revenues: $4.9 billion (vs. $4.6B Q1'25, $4.3B Q4'25)
- GAAP net loss: $229 million, or $0.42 per diluted share
- Adjusted net loss: $0.40 per diluted share (beat consensus of -$0.42 by $0.02)
- Adjusted EBITDA: $95 million, inclusive of an $80 million one-time energy cost from extreme cold weather
- Steel shipments: 4.1 million net tons (+~338,000 tons sequentially)
- FY2026 guidance maintained: ~16.5–17.0 million net tons of shipments
- Liquidity: $3.1 billion as of March 31, 2026
- Revenue consensus: ~$4.81 billion (beaten)
- CEO: Lourenco Goncalves

## Context facts

- FY2025 GAAP net loss: ~$1.4 billion, or $2.91 per diluted share
- Total debt: ~$7.66–7.7 billion (Q4 2025)
- Stelco acquisition completed November 1, 2024; EV ~$2.5 billion; $1.8 billion senior notes issued
- Post-earnings stock reaction: shares fell >6%, to ~$9.13 (secondary sources)
- Peers cited: Nucor (NUE), Steel Dynamics (STLD), U.S. Steel (X)
