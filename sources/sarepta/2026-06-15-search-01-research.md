Findings:
- Q1 2026 (reported May 6, 2026): net product revenues $330.5M (Elevidys $102.0M; PMO $228.6M); total revenues $730.8M; GAAP op income $358.4M, non-GAAP $397.7M; net income ~$331M; EPS $3.16. FY2026 guidance reiterated $1.2B–$1.4B. Cash & investments $748.3M at 3/31/26.
- Elevidys fell from ~$375M (Q1 2025) to $102.0M (~73% YoY) after the addressable population was limited to ambulatory DMD patients post-safety concerns.
- Three deaths from acute liver failure (2 Elevidys, 1 SRP-9004 Phase 1). FDA: boxed warning (ALI/ALF), removed nonambulatory eligibility, revoked AAVrh74 platform designation, clinical holds on SRP-9004/SRP-9003, voluntary U.S. shipment halt; later recommended removing hold for ambulatory patients — shipments resumed for ambulatory only.
- Restructuring: ~500 jobs / ~36% workforce cut; ~$400M annual savings target; pivot to siRNA platform.
- Stock down >80% over the year; market cap ~$3.7B (mid-2025) → ~$1.85B (June 18, 2026; shares ~$17.54).
- $40.0M Roche/Chugai Japan-launch milestone (March 2026); Roche pays mid-teens ex-U.S. royalties. Competitors: REGENXBIO (RGX-202, filing mid-2026), Solid Biosciences, Pfizer.
