## Safety / regulatory facts

- Three deaths attributed to acute liver failure: 2 Elevidys patients (nonambulatory) + 1 SRP-9004 LGMD Phase 1 patient (age 51)
- FDA added boxed warning for acute serious liver injury / acute liver failure to Elevidys
- Nonambulatory patients removed from Elevidys eligibility
- AAVrh74 viral vector platform designation revoked
- Clinical holds on SRP-9004 and SRP-9003 (bidridistrogene xeboparvovec)
- Voluntary U.S. shipment halt of all Elevidys; FDA later recommended removing hold for ambulatory patients; shipments resumed for ambulatory only

## Corporate / valuation facts

- Elevidys list price: $3.2M one-time treatment; first FDA-approved DMD gene therapy (June 22, 2023)
