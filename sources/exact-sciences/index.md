# Sources — Exact Sciences (EXAS) / Abbott buyout

## Primary facts (confirmed)

- Abbott acquired Exact Sciences for **$105.00/share cash**, ~**$21B** equity value, ~**$23B** enterprise value.
- Announced **Nov 20, 2025**; closed **March 23, 2026**; EXAS **delisted** from Nasdaq.
- EXAS FY2025 total revenue **$3.25B** (~$3.247B), up ~**18%**.
- FY2025 Screening revenue **$2.53B**, up ~**20%** (Cologuard + PreventionGenetics).
- FY2025 Precision Oncology revenue **$717M**.
- FY2025 **net loss ~$208M**; operating cash flow $491M; free cash flow $357M.
- Announcement-day stock move: ~**+18%**.
- 2021 post-COVID peak: ~**$150** (exit at $105 sits below this).
- Guardant **Shield**: first FDA-approved blood-based primary CRC screen (avg-risk, 45+); on **Quest** national network Q1 2026; CRC sensitivity ~**83%**, Stage I ~**55-65%**, misses majority of precancerous lesions.
- **Freenome SimpleScreen** advancing; **ACS 2026 guideline** reviews blood-based options vs Cologuard Plus.

## Note on a rejected figure

An early generic web-search summary returned a spurious "$1.62B quarterly revenue / +12% / $180M adjusted EBITDA" line that did not reconcile with primary sources (FY2025 revenue was $3.25B for the *full year*). This was treated as unreliable and excluded from the article.
