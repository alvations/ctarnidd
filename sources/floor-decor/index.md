# Sources — Floor & Decor (FND)

## Primary facts (Q1 FY2026, quarter ended March 26, 2026; reported April 30, 2026)

- Net sales: $1,152.3M, down 0.7% YoY.
- Comparable-store sales: -3.7% (transactions -5.5%, average ticket +1.9%).
- Monthly comps: January +0.4%, February -6.9%, March -4%.
- Diluted EPS: $0.37, down 17.8% from $0.45 prior-year quarter; missed $0.41 consensus.
- Net income: $39.7M, down 18.8% from $48.9M.
- Gross margin: 44.0%, up despite lower sales (strategic pricing vs. higher supply-chain costs).
- Stores: opened six warehouses; ended with 276 warehouse stores, 5 design studios, 5 distribution centers.
- Capital return: $400M share repurchase authorization.
- Q2 FY2026 comps trending ~-4.5% (per call).
- FY2026 guidance: net sales ~$4,770M–$4,990M; comps (4.0)% to flat; diluted EPS ~$1.83–$2.08; 20 new stores; 4%–7% sales-growth target.
- History: FY2024 comps -7.1%; Q1 FY2025 comps -1.8%; Q2 FY2025 comps +0.4% (first positive since Q4 FY2022).
- Valuation: ~$43 share price post ~16% drop; market cap ~$5.6B; trailing P/E ~24, forward ~22; specialty-retail avg forward ~11x; TTM revenue ~$4.68B, net income ~$199M, EPS ~$1.84.
- Mix: Pro sales ~50% of total vs. ~45% a year earlier.
- Housing backdrop: 2025 existing-home sales 4.06M, flat vs. 2024 (lowest since 1995); ~70% of mortgage holders below 5% entering 2026; 30-yr fixed ~6.19% in December; NAR projects +14% 2026 sales, Fannie Mae 4.46M.
