- Waymo: ~3,067 robotaxis (5th-gen driver); 500,000+ paid rides/week across 10 US cities; coverage expanded

- Pricing: Tesla avg $8.17/ride SF (vs Lyft $15.47, vs Waymo premium); Tesla wait ~15 min vs Waymo 5.7 min.

  Texas."
