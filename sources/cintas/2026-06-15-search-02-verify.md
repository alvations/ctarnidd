## Key findings

- Net income $502.5M, +8.4% (vs $463.5M). Operating income 23.2% of revenue vs 23.4% prior year; ex prior-year one-time gain, operating margin +40bps.
- Adjusted EPS guide excludes non-recurring UniFirst transaction expenses.
- Market cap ~$66.7B. Annual dividend $1.80/share, yield ~1.0%, 45 consecutive years of increases. $1B buyback authorization (Oct 28 2025); ~$1.45B returned to shareholders in the quarter.
- ~48,300 employees. Revenue tied to customer headcount and locations (wearer-count billing) — partly cyclical with US employment and business formation; more defensive than pure-cyclical industrials but not immune.
- UniFirst: final deal ~$5.5B announced March 11, 2026 ($275/share cash). Earlier proposal Jan 7, 2025 implied ~$5.3B; prior unsuccessful bids in 2022 and Jan 2025 (withdrawn March). Third attempt closed.
