## Key findings

- FQ3 fiscal 2026 reported March 25, 2026.
- Revenue $2.84B, +8.9% YoY (from $2.61B). Organic growth 8.2% (ex-acquisitions & FX).
- Gross margin $1.45B = 51.0% of revenue, an all-time high (vs 50.6% prior year, +40bps).
- Diluted EPS $1.24, +9.7% (vs $1.13).
- Raised FY26 guidance: revenue $11.21B–$11.24B; adjusted diluted EPS $4.86–$4.90.
- Trailing P/E ~36x (36.52x per public.com June 13 2026; 36.31x at $170.28 on TTM EPS $4.69). Forward P/E ~30.6x (GuruFocus, Mar 28 2026).
- Industry context: business-services median forward P/E ~12x; CTAS well above. CTAS P/E ~35x vs US Commercial Services ~22x.
