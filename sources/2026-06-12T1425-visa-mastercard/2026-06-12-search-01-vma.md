# Search 01 — Visa/Mastercard + stablecoins (read 2026-06-12)

Queries: "Visa Mastercard 2026 stablecoin threat GENIUS Act payments disruption interchange"; "Visa Mastercard Q1

FINANCIALS:
- Visa: net revenue ~**$10.9B** (+14.6%); operating margin ~**67%**; net margin ~54%; net income $6.0B. P/E
  ~**24.7x** (compressed from historical premium).
- Mastercard Q1 2026: net revenue **$8.4B** (+~16%); adj EPS $4.60 (+23%); operating margin ~**58%**.
- Combined: >**$20 trillion** annual payment volume; op margins >50%; zero receivables (capital-light); 60-yr
  two-sided network moat.

STABLECOIN THREAT:
- **GENIUS Act** (mid-2025): federal framework for payment stablecoins; lets retailers/tech firms/airlines issue
  dollar-backed stablecoins. Stablecoins settle peer-to-peer, BYPASS card interchange entirely.
- Merchants (Walmart, Amazon exploring own coins) want to escape ~2-3% interchange; Merchants Payments Coalition
  lobbying. V/MA stocks dropped **19-23%** on stablecoin fears. Bill Gurley: V/MA "heavily threatened."
- Stablecoin mkt cap → ~**$1T** by late 2026; tens of trillions on-chain volume.
- DEFENSE: Mastercard acquiring **BVNK** (stablecoin infra) for up to **$1.8B** (incl $300M contingent), closes
  late 2026; BVNK processes >$30B/yr. Visa has stablecoin partnerships. Strategy = own the bypass / settlement
  layer.
- OTHER bypass: real-time rails **UPI (India), Pix (Brazil), FedNow (US), SEPA Instant (EU)** already capture
  account-to-account volume that bypasses cards.
