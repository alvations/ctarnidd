Key findings:
- FY2025 (ended 3/31/2026): sales ¥12,479.6B (+4%), operating income ¥1,447.5B (+13%, record), net income ¥1,030.9B. Reported May 8, 2026.
- Closing quarter: revenue ¥3,036.4B (+8%); adjusted net income ¥83.1B vs ¥224.4B prior-year quarter; EPS ¥13.93 vs ¥37.04.
- PS5: 2.8M units in closing quarter (vs 4.5M YoY, 9.5M prior quarter); lifetime 93.7M as of 3/31/2026.
- Segments (closing quarter): Music +21.1% to ¥570B (OI +58.4%); Pictures +14.1% to ¥472.9B; I&SS +28.2% to ¥524.4B; G&NS weaker.
- G&NS FY2025: record OI ¥463.3B (+12%); revenue ~¥4.69 trillion; PSN MAU 132M (Dec 2025).
- Impairments: Bungie ¥120.1B; Sony Honda Mobility EV-exit ¥44.9B; Pixomondo ¥27.1B; total ~¥208B.
- Financial Services: partial spin-off Oct 2025; reclassified as discontinued; residual stake equity-method from Q3.
- FY2026 guidance: sales ¥12,300B (-1.4%); OI ¥1,600B (+10.5%); net income ¥1,160B (+12.5%); operating cash flow ¥1,500B.
- Capital: buyback raised to ¥500B; dividend up to ¥35/share; mid-range capital envelope raised to ¥5.7T from ¥4.8T.
- Outlook commentary: tariff uncertainty, memory/DRAM cost crunch, TSMC joint venture flagged.
