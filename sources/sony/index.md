## Primary facts (FY2025, ended March 31, 2026; reported May 8, 2026)

- Sales: ¥12,479.6 billion (+4% YoY), record.
- Operating income: ¥1,447.5 billion (+13% YoY), record.
- Net income attributable to stockholders: ¥1,030.9 billion.
- Closing-quarter revenue: ¥3,036.4 billion (+8% YoY).
- PS5 closing-quarter shipments: 2.8M units (4.5M YoY; 9.5M prior quarter). Lifetime: 93.7M as of 3/31/2026.
- G&NS FY2025 operating income: ¥463.3 billion (record, +12%); revenue ~¥4.69 trillion. PSN MAU 132M (Dec 2025).
- Bungie impairment: ¥120.1 billion (~$765M) — Destiny 2 / Marathon; second Bungie write-down.
- EV-exit (Sony Honda Mobility) losses: ¥44.9 billion. Pixomondo: ¥27.1 billion. Total impairments ~¥208 billion.
- Closing-quarter segments: Music +21.1% to ¥570B (OI +58.4%); Pictures +14.1% to ¥472.9B; I&SS +28.2% to ¥524.4B.
- Financial Services: partial spin-off Oct 2025, reclassified as discontinued; residual stake equity-method from Q3.
- FY2026 guidance: sales ¥12,300B (-1.4%); operating income ¥1,600B (+10.5%); net income ¥1,160B (+12.5%).
- Capital: buyback ¥500 billion (facility up to 230M shares, May 11 2026–May 10 2027); dividend raised; mid-range capital envelope raised to ¥5.7 trillion from ¥4.8 trillion.
