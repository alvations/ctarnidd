# Search 01 — AST SpaceMobile (read 2026-06-11)

SpaceMobile 2026 dilution losses market cap pre-revenue vs Rocket Lab Starlink direct-to-cell".

FINANCIALS:
- Q1 2026: revenue **$14.7M**; net loss to common **$191.0M**; total opex **$164.1M**. Cash **~$3.5B**
  (3/31/26).
- FY2026 guidance: **$150–200M** revenue (mobile-network partners + US govt); ~half from contracted backlog.
- 2025: revenue ~$70.92M; loss ~−$341.94M.
- Market cap ~**$26–36B** (June 2026) — pre/barely-revenue.

DILUTION:
- Shares outstanding **+437%** over 5 years; **~33%** dilution in the past year. FCF ~−$237M.

SATELLITES / TECH:
- Targeting ~**45 BlueBird** satellites in orbit during 2026; launch partners SpaceX, Blue Origin, others.
  (Block 2 BlueBird launches still ahead; deployment is a stated contingency for the revenue plan.)
- **98.9 Mbps** peak from in-orbit satellite to an **unmodified smartphone** over international waters.
- BlueBird 6: largest commercial comms array ever in LEO; >120 Mbps target.
- Partners: **AT&T and Verizon**.

COMPETITION:
- Starlink direct-to-cell (SpaceX): larger network, stronger balance sheet, own rockets; partners **T-Mobile**.
- AST antenna ~**35–40×** larger than Starlink's per-satellite DTC antenna → fewer sats to close link at
  broadband. AST "more focused."
- Rocket Lab (RKLB): real launch + space-systems revenue, more attractive on P/S; both early-stage / rich
  multiples.
