STOCK / LEADERSHIP:
- Plunged ~**50%** from >$600 to ~**$310**; mkt cap **$465B (Jan'25) → ~$300B (Dec'25)**, −~36%. +47% off March 2026 lows.
- May 2025: **suspended 2025 guidance**; CEO **Andrew Witty** departed abruptly; **Stephen Hemsley** (former CEO/chair) returned. Hemsley: insurers face "continuing public controversy over long-standing practices."
- Favorable preliminary **2027 MA rate** notice (+~9% stock pop). Sold **Optum UK** ($400M to UH Foundation); **$2B+ buyback** by end Q2 2026. Optum Health loss contracts → reserve.

DOJ:
- **Criminal AND civil** investigations into Medicare Advantage billing — whether UNH **inflated diagnoses** to
  trigger higher CMS reimbursement; deploying clinicians to increase diagnoses; extends to Optum Rx / physician
  reimbursement. UNH cooperating. No charges, no timeline.

CONTEXT: UnitedHealthcare CEO **Brian Thompson murdered Dec 4, 2024** in Manhattan — handle soberly; the public
reaction exposed deep hostility to the managed-care model (political/regulatory risk).
