- Record revenue: $7.91B, +~11% YoY, +~13% sequential
- GAAP gross margin 49.9%; non-GAAP gross margin 50.0% (highest in 25+ years)
- GAAP operating income $2.52B (31.9% of revenue); non-GAAP operating margin 32.1%
- GAAP EPS $3.51; non-GAAP EPS $2.86 (GAAP > non-GAAP — inversion driven by unrealized strategic-investment gains)
- Segments: Semiconductor Systems ~$6.0B; Applied Global Services (AGS) ~$1.7B
- Q3 FY2026 guidance: revenue ~$8.95B ±$500M; non-GAAP EPS $3.36 ±$0.20
- 2026 outlook raised: semiconductor-equipment business growth >30%; advanced packaging >50%
- China: ~24–27% of systems+services revenue in Q2 FY2026 (28% in FY2025; ~40% historically)
- Export-control impact: ~$600M FY2026 + ~$110M prior-Q4 unshipped ≈ ~$710M
- ICAPS / China expected "flat to slightly higher" for calendar 2026
- Besi (BE Semiconductor): ~9% stake acquired April 2025; hybrid-bonding collaboration for advanced packaging
- Valuation (as of mid-June 2026): P/S >16 (record), trailing P/E ~55, stock +~216% over trailing year
- Lead customer TSMC: 2026 capex guide $52–56B (+~32% YoY)
- Customer concentration: TSMC, Samsung, Intel
