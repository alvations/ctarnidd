# Search 01 — ASML monopoly + financials (read 2026-06-11)

Query: "ASML 2026 EUV lithography monopoly market cap revenue backlog China export controls High-NA bookings".

- Sole supplier of EUV lithography; Nikon & Canon exited EUV >10 years ago.
- Q1 2026 sales ~€8.8B at 53% gross margin; FY2026 guidance lifted to €36-40B despite China weakness.
- Year-end 2025 backlog €38.8B; €7.4B EUV bookings Q1 → multi-year visibility.
- 2030 outlook: €44-60B (High-NA ramp + HBM second cycle leg). [Another source framed ~€71B by 2030.]
- EUV never shipped to China; controls affect DUV; China fell to 19% of Q1 2026 system sales from 36% Q4 2025;
  impact concentrated in lower-margin DUV.
- Proposed MATCH Act would ban DUV exports to China.
- EUV machines >$200M; High-NA EUV ~$360-400M each.
