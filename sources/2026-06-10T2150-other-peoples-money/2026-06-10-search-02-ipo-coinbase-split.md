# Search 02 — IPO history + Coinbase revenue split (read 2026-06-10)

Queries: "Circle IPO June 2025 priced $31 surged peak $298 stock decline Coinbase distribution costs";
"Circle Coinbase revenue split 50% USDC reserve income distribution costs operating expenses Tether competition 2026".

IPO / price history:
- Priced $31.00 on June 4, 2025; began trading June 5, 2025 (NYSE: CRCL); valued ~$6.2B.
- Opened $69; intraday high $103.75; closed first day $83.23 (+168.48%). Biggest two-day pop since 1980 for $500M+ raises.
- All-time high $298.99 on June 23, 2025.
- Aug 2025 secondary: 10M Class A shares at $130.
- Now ~$79 (down ~75% from peak).

Coinbase revenue share:
- Coinbase earns 100% of interest on USDC held on its platform; 50% of residual reserve revenue on USDC held elsewhere.
- 2024: total distribution costs ~$1.01B, of which $907.9M paid to Coinbase = ~$0.54 of every dollar Circle earned.
- Agreement reviewed every 3 years; next review due 2026.
- Circle discloses Coinbase payments driven by Coinbase strategies it "neither controls nor oversees."

FY2025:
- Total revenue + reserve income $2.747B; reserve income $2.637B; other revenue $110M.
- Net loss attributable to common $70M; compensation expense ~$845M.

Competition / regulation:
- USDC second-largest stablecoin behind Tether USDT (~$140B vs ~$79B). USDC leads regulated/US/institutional venues.
- GENIUS Act prohibits stablecoin issuers paying interest to holders; invites regulated competitors.
