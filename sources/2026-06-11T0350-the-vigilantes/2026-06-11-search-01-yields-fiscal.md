# Search 01 — long-bond yields + fiscal (read 2026-06-11)

Query: "US 30-year Treasury yield 2026 5% term premium rising national debt $38 trillion deficit bond
vigilantes fiscal sustainability interest costs".

- 30Y Treasury >5.2% late May 2026 (highest in 19 years, since before 2008); 10Y ~4.7% (highest since 2007).
- Treasury sold $25B of 30Y bonds at 5% yield first time since 2007.
- Term premium (extra yield to hold long bonds) elevated; positive; fiscal concerns + global yields + oil keep
  upward pressure.
- Interest ~3.25% of GDP and ~19% of federal revenue in FY2025.
- CRFB: if yields stay ~55bps above CBO projections, interest grows ~2.5x, $880B → $2.5T by 2036, ~30% of
  revenue; warns of "fiscal crisis."
