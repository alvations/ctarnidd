# Sources — FEMSA (FMX) Q1 2026

Article: `2026-06-21T0930-femsa.md` · Reported: April 30, 2026 (Q1 2026)

## Primary facts (CONFIRMED)

- Net consolidated income: Ps. 17,639M, +97.3% YoY (boosted by one-time BradyPLUS–Imperial Dade gain).
- Net income ex one-time gain: Ps. 5,688M, -36.4% YoY (higher net financing expenses + absence of discontinued-operations income).
- Total revenue: +6.1% reported / +8.5% comparable (peso appreciation drag).
- Operating income: +5.5% reported / +12.1% comparable.
- Gross profit +6.6%; gross margin ~40.5%.
- Net majority income: Ps. 4.34 per FEMSA Unit (+167.9%); US$2.41 per ADS.
- Oxxo Mexico: revenue +8.3%, op income +20.9%, SSS +6.0%, gross profit +11.5%.
- Coca-Cola FEMSA: revenue +1.1% to ~Ps. 70,925M (~US$3.9B), op income -2.3%, gross profit +4.5%.
- Americas & Mobility: revenue +12.9% reported, op income +34.0% reported.
- Spin by Oxxo: 11.0M active users (+22.3%); Spin Premia 28.4M (+12.8%); digital tender 50.6% vs 42.5%.
- Stock reaction: +5.56% on announcement day.
- FEMSA Forward: divested full Heineken/Heineken Holding stake (~14.76% end-2022) via share sales + exchangeable bonds; sold plastics-solutions ops to AMMI; proceeds to debt reduction + shareholder returns.

## Note on contested figure

Absolute Q1 revenue: FEMSA IR confirms +6.1%/+8.5% growth but does not state the absolute total in the press release; one source cited Ps. 207,784M on a different consolidation basis, another Ps. 149.3B. Article uses growth percentages only (defensible, confirmed).
