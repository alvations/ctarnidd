- Reported net sales: $3,712M, +5% YoY reported; organic +2% YoY.
- GAAP net income: $89M; GAAP diluted EPS: $0.24.
- Adjusted diluted EPS: $0.91.
- GAAP operating margin: 6.7%; adjusted operating margin: 15.0%; gross margin: 76.4% (+140 bps).
