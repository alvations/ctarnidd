# Search 01 — research (Cloudflare)

Query: Cloudflare NET Q1 2026 earnings revenue growth net income margin valuation guidance.

## Findings used
- Revenue $639.8M (+34%); FY26 guide ~$2.81B (~30%); Q2 guide $664–665M.
- Non-GAAP net income $94M; non-GAAP operating income $73.1M (11.4% margin, +31% YoY).
- Gross margin 71.2% (GAAP), down from prior — flagged for verification (turned out: non-GAAP 73% from 77%).
- 4,416 customers >$100K/yr; $1M+ deals +73% YoY. FCF $84.1M (13% of revenue).
- 20% workforce reduction (~1,100 employees) to pivot to agentic-AI operating model.

## Forensic angle selected
Priced-for-perfection + the falling-gross-margin tell. A premium-software multiple (~28x sales) assumes pristine
expanding margins; Cloudflare's gross margin is sliding as it scales into capital-intensive AI inference/compute —
the next leg of growth carries worse unit economics. Plus non-GAAP-vs-GAAP gap, hyperscaler competition, FCF
capital-intensity risk, layoff-dressed-as-confidence, Fastly cautionary tale.
