## Primary facts (confirmed)

Q1 fiscal 2026, reported June 2, 2026 (period ended May 2, 2026):
- Net sales: +11.1% to ~$3.16 billion (est. ~$3.10B).
- Comparable sales: +5.3% (StreetAccount est. ~4.6%); average ticket +3.7%, transactions +1.6%.
- Operating income: +11.6% to $448.3 million = 14.2% of net sales.
- Diluted EPS: $7.74, +15.5% (est. ~$6.86).
- Gross margin: 40.1% vs 39.1% prior year (lower inventory shrink, higher merchandise margin).

Full-year FY2026 guidance (updated June 2, 2026):
- Net sales growth 6%–7% (unchanged).
- Comparable sales growth 2.5%–3.5% (UNCHANGED — less than half the Q1 5.3%).
- Operating income growth 6.5%–9% (low end raised from 6%).
- Diluted EPS $28.36–$28.80 (raised from $28.05–$28.55).

Context:
- FY2025 full-year comps +5.4% (ticket +3.3%, transactions +2.0%); operating income ~$1.5B = ~12.4% of net sales.
- Operating margin peaked near ~16% (full-year) in the post-pandemic surge (FY2022 era), compressing since.
- Stock ~$509 late May 2026; trailing P/E low-to-mid 20s.
- Competitive backdrop: Sephora-at-Kohl's shop-in-shop network; Ulta's own Ulta-at-Target partnership maturing.
