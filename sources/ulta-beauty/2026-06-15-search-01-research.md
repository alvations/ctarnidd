Key findings:
- Latest reported quarter is Q1 FY2026 (period ended May 2, 2026), reported June 2, 2026. Original assignment premise (comps decelerating to LSD already, margin off peak) is PARTLY correct on margin/guide but the latest quarter was a BEAT: comps +5.3%, net sales +11.1%, EPS $7.74 (+15.5%). PIVOTED thesis to the beat-vs-guide divergence: full-year comp guide held at 2.5%–3.5% despite the 5.3% print.
- Operating margin: peaked ~16% (full-year) in post-pandemic surge; FY2024 ~13.9%; FY2025 ~12.4% (operating income ~$1.5B). One source cited 18.7% but that reads as a quarterly peak — used "roughly 16%" full-year framing.
- Stock ~$508.85 on May 29, 2026; trailing P/E ~23.3x. Forward P/E mid-to-high teens to low-20s depending on date.
- Sephora-at-Kohl's and Ulta-at-Target shop-in-shop dynamics confirmed as the competitive frame.
