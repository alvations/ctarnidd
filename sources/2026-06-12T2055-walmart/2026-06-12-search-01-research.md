# Search 01 — research (Walmart)

Query: Walmart Q1 fiscal 2027 earnings advertising revenue Walmart Connect growth ecommerce profit valuation.

## Findings used
- Total revenue $177.75B (+7.3%); US comps +4.1% (despite ~100bps pharmacy pricing headwind).
- Advertising +30%+ globally; Walmart US ad +36%; Walmart Connect US +44% ex-VIZIO; marketplace ad spend +50%.
- Membership fee revenue +17% enterprise; memberships ~$2.07B.
- Global online sales +26%; e-commerce reached profitability.
- Membership + advertising ≈ one-third of operating income; adjusted operating income +~5% constant currency.

## Forensic angle selected
Denominator/proportion illusion + multiple re-rating: the dazzling 30–44% growth is only ~1/3 of operating income;
consolidated AOI grew ~5% cc. Walmart rerated to a tech-like multiple on the fast third while the slow
two-thirds (low-margin retail) still dominates. Retail media = ceilinged margin layer, not infinite TAM;
multiple-expansion drove the stock and can't repeat; Costco-membership analogy double-edged; trade-down cyclical.
