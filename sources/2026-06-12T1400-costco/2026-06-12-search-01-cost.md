Q3 FY2026:
- Net sales **$69.15B** (+11.6%); total revenue $70.53B. Comparable sales +9.8% (+**6.6% ex gas/FX**); digital
  comps +21.5%.
- **Membership fee income $1.37B** (+10.7%); annualized ~$5.3B. Net income **$2.19B** / **$4.93** dil EPS (from
  $1.90B / $4.28).
- Renewal: **89.7% worldwide**, **92.2% US/Canada** (one source: 93% record). **148.5M** cardholders.

VALUATION:
- P/E ~**51-52 trailing**, ~**46-50 forward** (one source ~57). S&P median ~**23.7x**. Priced "like a high-growth
  tech company."

BUSINESS MODEL:
- Merchandise gross margin ~**11.0%**; after warehouse ops/labor/SG&A almost nothing left from merchandise →
  operating income comes **primarily from membership fee income** (margins ~**75-80%**).
- Renewal: first analyst flags of forward softening from MIX SHIFT (digital/promo members renew slightly lower).
