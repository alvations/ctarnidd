## Notes on thesis pivot

Suggested angle assumed "traffic roughly flat... soft organic comp." Most recent quarter shows LongHorn +7.2% and Olive Garden +3.2% — comp is NOT flat. Pivoted to an accurate, defensible thesis: the 4.2% comp is price-led (~3.5% check), underlying dine-in traffic is near-flat-to-negative once catering is removed, bought/built units inflate total sales, margins compress at the flagship, and ~22x is a full multiple for a mature, cyclical casual-dining base.
