# Sources — Paycom (PAYC)

## Primary facts (confirmed)

- Q1 2026 (reported May 7, 2026): revenue $571.9M, +7.8% YoY; recurring & other >95% of sales.
- GAAP net income $155.7M; GAAP diluted EPS $3.04 (vs $2.48 PY). Non-GAAP net income $161.3M; non-GAAP EPS $3.15 (beat ~$565M consensus).
- Adjusted EBITDA $275.4M, margin 48.2%. Operating income $210.2M (36.8% margin).
- FY2026 guidance (reaffirmed after Q1 beat): revenue $2.175B–$2.195B (~6.5% midpoint); adj. EBITDA $950M–$970M (~44% midpoint margin).
- Q1 buyback: 8.38M shares for ~$1.07B; $17.7M dividends; new $2B authorization.
- Revenue growth history: 2021 ~25%, 2022 ~30% (peak), 2023 ~23%, 2024 ~11%, 2025 ~9% (FY2025 ~$2.05B). Q1 2026 +7.8%.
- Beti cannibalization: disclosed Q3 2023 call by founder/CEO Chad Richison; Q3 2023 rev $406.3M vs prior $410–412M; FY2023 guide cut to $1.679–1.684B from $1.715–1.717B. Subsequent deemphasis of Beti; Chris Thomas elevated to Co-CEO.
- Founder control: Richison founded Paycom 1998, CEO throughout, Chairman since Aug 2016; multi-million-share holder direct + indirect (Ernest Group, family trusts).
- Valuation (mid-2026): forward P/E ~12.6 (GuruFocus, Jun 7) to ~15.5 across providers; software median ~18.6; EV/EBITDA ~15.4.
