# Search 02 — economic impact / recession risk (read 2026-06-11)

Query: "2026 Iran war oil price $120 Brent economic impact recession inflation Strait of Hormuz tankers
shipping rates spare capacity OPEC".

- Strait closed ~Mar 4 2026 → oil + LNG stranded → Brent surged past $120 (from ~$72 Feb 27).
- 1970s energy-crisis echoes: acute shortages, inflation, stagflation/recession risk.
- Oxford Economics: oil ~$140/bbl for ~2 months → mild recession in parts of global economy.
- ~200 ships wandering the region; supply chains disrupted.
- ECB postponed planned rate cuts Mar 19, raised 2026 inflation forecast, cut GDP; warned energy-intensive
  economies face technical-recession risk if blockade persists through summer.
- OPEC pledged to ramp production but increase "largely symbolic," falls short of lost supply.
- CNBC: "misplaced euphoria / markets sleepwalking into recession amid oil shock."
