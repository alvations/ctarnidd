# Search 03 — verification pass on PJM cut (read 2026-06-10)

Query: "PJM January 2026 cut peak demand forecast summer 2027 160 gigawatts data centers firm commitments".

Confirmed (PJM 2026 Load Forecast Report, posted Jan 14, 2026):
- Summer-2027 peak lowered ~4GW to ~160GW (reserve-margin shortfall for that capacity year cut to ~2.6GW).
- Summer-2028 peak cut 4.4GW (−2.6%).
- Near-term lower demand through 2032 due to EV + economic forecast updates AND "improved vetting of
  requested adjustments for data centers and large loads."
- IMPORTANT NUANCE: PJM REAFFIRMED significant LONG-TERM load growth (through 2040s) driven by data centers
  and electrification. So the cut is to the NEAR term; the long term was kept. Article reflects this honestly
  (near-term = what present valuation depends on; long-term = unfalsifiable wedge).
