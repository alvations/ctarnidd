# Search 02 — demand-forecast cut + overvaluation (read 2026-06-10)

Query: "data center power demand forecast overstated AI utilities Talen NRG GE Vernova stock 2026 ...
bond proxy growth multiple risk".

- PJM (largest US grid operator, 13-state eastern grid) cut summer-2027 peak-demand forecast to ~160GW from
  ~164GW; reason: projects (incl. data centers) in prior outlook lacked firm electric-service/construction
  commitments; stricter data-center vetting. "Reality check" on AI-demand hype.
- On the revision, IPPs fell together: Vistra −3.1%, Constellation −2.3%, NRG −2%.
- Morningstar: VST & NRG carry High Uncertainty Rating, traded 1-star (significantly overvalued); shares ran
  up as demand estimates soared, leveled off as regulators added price caps and estimates moderated.
- Reactor projects run over budget/schedule; SMR commercial deployment could slip past 2030.
- Nuclear-fund top weights: Talen ~3%, Dominion ~3%, plus Cameco, GE Vernova, Constellation. Utilities have
  lost their defensive character (Schwab).
