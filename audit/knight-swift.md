# Audit — Knight-Swift Earned Nine Cents and Lost Money on GAAP — But the Stock Prices a Full Recovery

Article: `2026-06-18T1500-knight-swift.md` · Ticker: KNX · Sector: transportation

## Method

Forensic thesis: **cyclical-priced-as-secular** with a **quality-of-earnings (adjusted-vs-GAAP)** overlay. Knight-Swift, North America's largest truckload carrier, reported Q1 2026 (quarter ended March 31, 2026; results released April 16, 2026) with a GAAP diluted loss of $(0.01) per share versus $0.19 a year earlier, and adjusted EPS of $0.09 versus $0.28. The consolidated adjusted operating ratio worsened ~230 bps year over year to 97%, and the LTL segment swung to an operating loss. The market prices the full-year adjusted EPS guidance of $0.45–$0.49 and a normalized up-cycle recovery, awarding a recovery/peak multiple to a price-taking cyclical still printing trough results. Supporting frames: denominator illusion (large % EPS growth off a depressed base), commodity price-taker (no escape from the spot rate market), moat-vs-cycle (LTL diversification failed to de-risk and instead amplified the bad quarter), and priced-for-perfection asymmetry (recovery already in the price; trough-extension risk is not).

## Fact-check

CONFIRMED against the SEC 8-K (exhibit 99, filed April 16, 2026), company IR earnings PDF, Investing.com, StockTitan, and the Motley Fool transcript:

- GAAP Q1 2026 diluted EPS: **$(0.01)** (net loss attributable to Knight-Swift of ~$1.3M); prior-year Q1 2025 GAAP EPS **$0.19**. CONFIRMED.
- Adjusted Q1 2026 EPS: **$0.09**; prior-year Q1 2025 adjusted EPS **$0.28**. CONFIRMED. (Consensus was ~$0.17; result missed.)
- Total revenue: **$1,850M (~$1.85B), +1.4% YoY**; revenue excluding fuel surcharge +0.3% to ~$1,638M. CONFIRMED.
- Consolidated adjusted operating ratio: **97%, ~+230 bps YoY (worse)**. CONFIRMED.
- One-time items (~$45M aggregate): **$18.0M** adverse LTL claims development tied to a 2022 arbitration ruling; **$4.1M** adverse Mexico VAT reimbursement decision; **$12–14M** net weather + fuel impact; deferred warehousing projects. CONFIRMED.
- LTL segment: swung from a **~$12.7M operating profit** (Q1 2025) to a **~$3.6M operating loss**; LTL adjusted operating ratio ~**99.6%**. CONFIRMED.
- Truckload: revenue per loaded mile (ex-fuel, ex-intersegment) **+1.4% YoY**; loaded miles **−1.8%**. CONFIRMED.
- Full-year 2026 adjusted EPS guidance: **$0.45–$0.49**. CONFIRMED.
- Shares outstanding: ~**162.3M** (end of 2025). CONFIRMED.
