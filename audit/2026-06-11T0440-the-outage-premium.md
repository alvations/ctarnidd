## Method

Original market-research piece, not investment advice. Forensic thesis: CrowdStrike caused the largest IT
outage in history (July 2024) and the market responded by bidding it to record highs and a near-hundred-times
forward multiple — the purest illustration of late-cycle "forgive everything" psychology in the series. The
recovery is the warning: a perfection multiple on a company that proved catastrophic, kernel-level, monoculture
error is possible; live Delta liability; Microsoft-Defender bundling squeeze; margin for error ~zero. Verified.

## Fact-check / verification (pre-fact-check `29232ed` → published; title "The Outage Premium" held)

Verified as drafted:
- July 19, 2024 outage: flawed Falcon update crashed **~8.5M Windows** systems; largest IT outage in history;
  kernel-level boot-loop requiring manual remediation.
- Delta: ~**$500M** claimed loss, **7,000+** flights canceled, ~**1.3M** passengers, 40,000 servers reset;
  Delta v. CRWD **continues** (Georgia judge let most of case proceed, May 2025).
- **Shareholder** suit **dismissed** Jan 14, 2026 (Judge Pitman, Austin) — distinct from the live customer suit.
- FY2026 revenue ~**$4.8B**, growth >**20%**; Microsoft Defender (E5 bundle) + Palo Alto bundling pressure;
  best-in-breed esp. non-Windows.
