## Method

Original market-research piece, not investment advice. Forensic thesis: PANW's dazzling growth (rev +31%, NGS ARR
+60%, RPO $18.4B) is partly BOUGHT, not earned — ~half the ARR growth is from acquisitions (CyberArk +
Chronosphere ~$1.6B; organic NGS ARR +28%, organic RPO +22%), and the organic part is won with platformization
GIVEAWAYS (free periods/discounts), whose receipt is the DECLINING gross margin (2 yrs). ARR/RPO are the most-
easily-flattered forward metrics; weight the grounded revenue (+24%). Two big integrations at once = execution
risk (mgmt: "ahead of plan"). ~50x fwd P/E / PEG ~4 / premium P/S; "discount to CrowdStrike" anchors on the
priciest comp. Honest-nuance: secular non-discretionary market, real platformization moat, strong FCF (~38%),
$18B RPO visibility, respected CEO — a dominant land-grabber priced as a clean compounder.
