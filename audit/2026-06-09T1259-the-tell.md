## Method

Original market-research piece, not a published news report or investment advice. The Bloomberg
seed ("CoreWeave Founders Have Dumped $2.3 Billion in Stock Since IPO", 2026-06-09) was paywalled
(403); the story was reconstructed from non-paywalled coverage, SEC filing summaries, and
independent research, then written as an original piece with a different title and angle. Every
figure was checked against the sources below; where they conflicted, the most authoritative
(SEC-filing-derived) figure was used; unverifiable claims were cut.

## Corrections (pre-fact-check draft `5bd7735` → published version)

- **Stock return:** "roughly tripling from IPO price" → **up ~150%** from the $40 IPO (now ~$102); the spring **peak near $138** was ~3.4×, but the current level is a ~150% gain, not a triple.
- **Nvidia stake:** "roughly 6%" → **~11%** (~$4.9B), risen from 6.3% via 2025–26 investments.
- **Depreciation stat:** swapped the contested "$1.14B in a single quarter ≈55% of revenue" for the better-sourced **FY2025 D&A of ~$2.45B ≈ ~48% of $5.13B revenue.**
- **Net loss added:** **$1.17B (2025)**, widening from **$863M (2024)** — sharper than the draft's vaguer "loses money."
- **Debt framing:** the draft's "$17B long-term / mid-$20Bs total" replaced with the confirmed **~$28B equity+debt raised in 12 months to Mar 2026, a $4.2B 2026 maturity, capex $31–35B, double-digit debt/equity.**
- **Meta contract:** "$14.2B through 2031" → the more recent **$21B (April 2026)**; added **Jane Street $6B**.
- **Backlog:** "near $100 billion" → **$66.8B at year-end 2025, swelling toward $95–100B** with 2026 deals.
- **Venturo:** specified **chief strategy officer** and **second-largest US insider seller by value this year** (Washington Service).
- **Timing:** clarified sales are **since the August 2025 lockup expiry** (the Bloomberg headline frames it "since IPO").
- **Magnetar:** the single-sourced "$5.5B / 9.7%" softened to "selling in a steady drip for months… reported to have carried billions out and cut its stake below ten percent," consistent with the granular Form-4 record.
