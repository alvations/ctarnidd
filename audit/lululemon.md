# Audit — Lululemon Grows Revenue 4% While Profit Collapses 37% and the Americas Bleed

Article: `2026-06-17T1200-lululemon.md` · Ticker: LULU · Sector: apparel

## Method

Forensic thesis: Lululemon's Q1 fiscal 2026 print (quarter ended May 3, 2026, reported June 4, 2026) is a denominator-illusion story. A 4% headline revenue gain (2% constant currency) masks an organic stall — Americas comparable sales negative for a fifth straight quarter — while China alone carries consolidated growth. Beneath the top line, income from operations fell 37% and gross margin compressed 410 bps, with the appearance of **markdowns** in the margin bridge signaling demand softness (not just tariffs) and a leaking pricing moat. Frames deployed: denominator illusion (bought/FX growth vs negative comps); customer/geographic concentration (China); quality-of-earnings (revenue up, operating profit down 37%); moat-vs-loophole (Alo/Vuori competition); cyclical-priced-as-secular; guidance cut twice; inventory tell (units -4%); leadership uncertainty (interim CEO). Mandatory honest-nuance section concedes profitability, real China growth, tariff-driven (reversible) margin hit, lean inventory, and a stock already down 40%+ YTD.

## Fact-check

CONFIRMED against SEC Form 8-K (lulu-20260503xex991.htm), lululemon corporate press release (06-04-2026), CNBC, StockTitan, Investing.com, SGI Europe:

- Q1 FY2026 net revenue: ~$2.47B ($2.5B rounded in press), up 4% (2% constant currency). CONFIRMED.
- Income from operations: $276.9M, down 37%. CONFIRMED.
- Operating margin: 11.2%, down 730 bps. CONFIRMED.
- Net income: $195M vs $314.6M prior year. CONFIRMED.
- Diluted EPS: $1.69 vs $2.60 prior year. CONFIRMED.
- Gross margin: 54.2%, down 410 bps; tariffs ~280 bps of product-margin hit, ~100 bps efficiency offset, markdowns ~40 bps. CONFIRMED.
- Comparable sales: +1% reported / -2% constant dollar (company). CONFIRMED.
- Americas: revenue ~-3% (-4% cc) to ~$1.6B; comps -5% to -6%; fifth straight quarter of declines. CONFIRMED.
- China Mainland: revenue +30% (+23% cc) to $478.4M; comps +13% cc. CONFIRMED.
- Inventory: $1.7B, +2% dollars, -4% units. CONFIRMED.
- FY2026 guidance: revenue $11.0B–$11.15B (flat to down ~1%); diluted EPS $10.95–$11.15; cut from prior ~$11.35B–$11.50B / ~$14 EPS. CONFIRMED.
- Q2 guidance: gross margin ~-410 bps; EPS $1.76–$1.81. CONFIRMED.
- Interim CEO Meghan Frank "negative commentary on social channels" quote. CONFIRMED.
- Stock down ~40%+ YTD at print. CONFIRMED (TheStreet "41% YTD skid").

Corrections applied during fact-check: prior-year net income harmonized to $314.6M (official 8-K) from a $314.5M secondary source; comparable-sales sentence clarified to show both reported (+1%) and constant-dollar (-2%) bases.
