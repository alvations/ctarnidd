## Method
Forensic thesis: WING was priced like software (~80-100x at peak) on its asset-light royalty model + hypergrowth
comps, but Q1 2026 domestic same-store sales FELL 8.7% (from +0.5%) — the comp engine that earns the multiple went
into reverse. Revenue +7.4%/EBITDA +9.9% still grew, but only on new units (97) + royalties on a bigger system. The
multiple has already begun de-rating (stock down ~15% to ~$145, forward P/E ~30x, TTM ~37.5x) but is still ~2x the
hospitality sector (~17-21x) and above fair value — de-rating underway, possibly unfinished. Frames: comp-reversal;
premium-still-deflating; unit-growth-carries-it (depends on franchisee health a comp decline threatens); franchisor-
insulation hides franchisee squeeze; chicken-cost volatility; weather/gas transitory-vs-structural + H2-recovery
hope; premium-multiple asymmetry. Honest-nuance: best-in-class asset-light model, ~$2M AUV, high digital mix, 97
openings, lapped a huge prior comp, weather/gas real, has reaccelerated before.
