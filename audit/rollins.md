# Audit — Rollins Trades Near 50 Times Earnings on 6.6% Organic Growth as Bought Deals Pad the Topline

## Method

Forensic thesis: Rollins (NYSE: ROL), parent of Orkin, is a high-quality, recurring-revenue, defensive pest-control compounder priced near 50x trailing / ~44x forward earnings — a software-style multiple — while its underlying organic growth is only mid-single digits (6.6% in Q1 2026). The double-digit reported topline (10.2%) is materially padded by serial, tolerant-priced acquisitions (~2-3% of growth from M&A by management's own framework). Frames deployed: bought-growth-masks-organic-stall; cyclical/discretionary-priced-as-secular; adjusted-vs-GAAP quality-of-earnings (flat GAAP EPS vs +9.1% adjusted EPS); margin compression in a "growth" quarter; denominator illusion in the price-increase story (half of organic growth is price); roll-up treadmill / thinning target pool; priced-for-perfection asymmetry. Honest-nuance section concedes recurring/sticky revenue, strong free cash flow, real route-density M&A logic, genuine pricing power, and scarcity of profitable defensive compounders.

## Fact-check

CONFIRMED figures (Q1 2026, reported April 23, 2026; press release / 8-K):
- Revenue: $906.4 million, +10.2% YoY — CONFIRMED.
- Organic revenue growth: 6.6% — CONFIRMED.
- GAAP diluted EPS: $0.22, flat YoY — CONFIRMED.
- Adjusted EPS: $0.24, +9.1% — CONFIRMED.
- Operating income: $145M (+2.0%); operating margin 16.1%, down ~120 bps — CONFIRMED.
- Adjusted operating income: $153M (+4.0%); adjusted operating margin 16.9%, down ~100 bps — CONFIRMED.
- Gross margin down ~60 bps (lower early-quarter volume, insurance/claims) — CONFIRMED.
- Service-line growth: residential +9.3%, commercial +9.6%, termite/ancillary +13.5% (reported); organic residential ~4.2%, commercial ~7.7%, termite/ancillary ~10% — CONFIRMED.
- FY2026 guidance: 7-8% organic + 2-3% M&A = 9-11% total; price +3-4%, outpacing CPI — CONFIRMED.
- Romex Pest Control acquisition (2026) — CONFIRMED.
- 2025 acquisitions: Fox Pest Control, Saela Pest Control (closed April 2025); ~$310M deployed on acquisitions in 2025 — CONFIRMED.
- 2025 operating cash flow $678.1M (+11.6%); free cash flow ~$650M (+12.1%) — CONFIRMED.
- Valuation: trailing P/E ~49.6, forward P/E ~43-44; forward P/E ~234% above personal-services industry median (~13.2); PEG ~3.44 — CONFIRMED (as of mid-April 2026).
