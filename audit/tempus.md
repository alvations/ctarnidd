# Audit — Tempus AI grows revenue 36% to $348M yet GAAP losses widen to $126M

Article: `2026-06-17T1100-tempus.md` · Ticker: TEM · Sector: healthcare

## Method

Forensic thesis: Tempus AI is priced as a secular AI/genomics compounder but reports as a fast-growing, GAAP-unprofitable diagnostics lab whose losses are widening faster than revenue. The article applies several forensic frames against the verified Q1 2026 (reported May 5, 2026) results:

- **Adjusted-vs-GAAP / quality-of-earnings:** GAAP net loss of $125.9M grew ~85% YoY while revenue grew 36%; the gap between guided ~$65M adjusted EBITDA and the GAAP loss is filled by stock comp and bought growth.
- **Stock-comp dilution:** $56.3M of quarterly SBC (incl. related employer payroll taxes) is added back to manufacture adjusted profitability.
- **Bought-growth-masks-organic:** Ambry Genetics ($375M cash + ~4.8M shares, closed Feb 3, 2025) inflated the consolidated growth rate (~79% for FY2025 guided).
- **Moat-vs-loophole / name-vs-revenue:** Spruce Point's May 2025 short report alleging the AI label overstates a business mostly built on reimbursed diagnostics — labeled throughout as ALLEGATION, not fact; Tempus disputes it and the stock recovered ~15%.
- **Related-party revenue:** Spruce Point's Pathos AI / ~$200M TCV related-party allegation — labeled as allegation, not adjudicated.
- **Commodity-price-taker / reimbursement floor; denominator (dilution); priced-for-perfection asymmetry.**

Honest-nuance section concedes: broad-based growth, MRD ~500% volume growth, improving adj. EBITDA trajectory, ~$764M cash runway, genuine multimodal data moat, Merck/pharma arms-length demand.

## Fact-check

CONFIRMED figures (primary: Tempus IR press release / 8-K / 10-Q, May 5 2026; corroborated by StockTitan, Businesswire, MarketBeat, Investing.com):

- Q1 2026 revenue: **$348.1M**, up ~36.1% YoY — CONFIRMED.
- GAAP net loss Q1 2026: **$125.9M** (vs **$68.0M** prior-year quarter) — CONFIRMED. StockTitan cites $125,919K.
- Stock-based compensation + related employer payroll taxes: **$56.3M** — CONFIRMED (refined wording to include "and related employer payroll taxes" per company disclosure).
- Unrealized losses on marketable securities: **$32.3M** — CONFIRMED.
- Diagnostics revenue: **$261.1M**, +34.7%; Oncology volume +28%; Hereditary volume +54% — CONFIRMED.
- Data and Applications revenue: **$87.0M**, +40.5%; Insights +44.1% — CONFIRMED.
- MRD volume ~6,500 tests, up ~500% YoY — CONFIRMED.
- FY2026 guidance: **$1.59–$1.60B** revenue (~25% growth), ~**$65M** adjusted EBITDA — CONFIRMED.
- Ambry Genetics: closed Feb 3, 2025; $375.0M cash + ~4,843,136 Class A shares; ~$102.6M Q3'25 revenue contribution; FY2025 consolidated ~$1.24B (~79% growth) — CONFIRMED.
- Cash ~$764M and convertible debt ~$468M (late 2025) — CONFIRMED (period-labeled "late 2025" in article).
- 2024 AI-related revenue ~$12.4M / total ~$693.4M, and 50–60% downside — these are **Spruce Point ALLEGATIONS**, labeled as such, not asserted as fact.
- Stock ~$55–$56 after the print ($55.81 close cited) — CONFIRMED.
- Eric Lefkofsky / Groupon background — public record; governance characterization attributed to Spruce Point.

No invented numbers. All allegations explicitly labeled. Contested AI-revenue figure attributed to Spruce Point.
