# Audit — Snap's ad engine grew 3% while Meta's grew 33%, and the gap is the whole story

Article: `2026-06-17T0830-snap.md` · Ticker: SNAP · Sector: social

## Method

Forensic thesis: Snap's Q1 2026 (reported May 6, 2026) was sold as a turnaround — total revenue +12% to $1,529M, DAU back to growth at 483M, net loss narrowed from $140M to $89M. The forensic move is to decompose the 12%: advertising revenue (the core business) grew only 3% to ~$1.24B, while "other revenue" (Snapchat+ subscriptions plus a now-terminated Perplexity AI deal) grew 87% to $285M and carried the headline. The same quarter, Meta's family-of-apps ad revenue grew 33% — a roughly tenfold gap in the one metric that matters, evidencing a price-taker losing share in an auction set by a giant. Supporting frames: denominator illusion (DAU growth concentrated in low-ARPU Rest-of-World; global ARPU $3.17 vs North America $9.23); quality-of-earnings (Q1 SBC + related payroll of $263M exceeds the $233M Adjusted EBITDA it is excluded from; eight years public with no GAAP annual profit); all-in capital allocation (16% workforce cut, ~1,000 roles, $500M+ annual savings, to fund $2,195 Specs AR glasses); priced-for-perfection asymmetry; cyclical-dressed-as-secular (brand-ad exposure; ~$20–25M/month Middle East geopolitical drag).

## Fact-check

CONFIRMED against Snap 8-K (SEC, filed 2026-05-06), company IR, StockTitan, CNBC, Yahoo Finance:
- Q1 2026 total revenue $1,529M, +12% YoY — CONFIRMED.
- Advertising revenue +3% YoY to ~$1.24B — CONFIRMED.
- Other revenue +87% YoY to $285M — CONFIRMED.
- Net loss $89M (improved from $140M prior-year) — CONFIRMED.
- Adjusted EBITDA $233M (more than doubled) — CONFIRMED.
- Operating cash flow $327M; free cash flow $286M — CONFIRMED.
- DAU 483M (+5%); MAU 956M; global ARPU $3.17 (+7% from $2.96) — CONFIRMED.
- North America ARPU $9.23 (+10%) — CONFIRMED.
- SBC + related payroll $263M in Q1; FY2026 SBC guided ~$1.05B — CONFIRMED.
- Meta family-of-apps ad revenue ~$55B, +33% YoY (Q1 2026) — CONFIRMED.
- Perplexity deal (reported up to $400M) ended in quarter; Q2 guidance assumes zero Perplexity contribution — CONFIRMED.
- Q2 2026 guidance: revenue $1.52–1.55B; Adjusted EBITDA $175–200M — CONFIRMED.
- ~16% workforce cut (~1,000 employees), 300+ open roles closed, $500M+ annual savings; Specs AR glasses at $2,195, shipping fall 2026 in US/UK/France — CONFIRMED.
- ~$20–25M/month Middle East geopolitical ad-revenue drag (March) — CONFIRMED (company-disclosed estimate; labeled as estimate in article).
- Snap IPO March 2017, no full-year GAAP profit since — CONFIRMED (consistent with public filing history).

Corrections made during drafting: none required; angle premise (perennially GAAP-unprofitable, ad business growing far slower than Meta, heavy SBC dilution, low-ARPU user concentration) held against the latest quarter. Calibrated to concede the genuine Q1 improvements (DAU return, FCF, narrowing loss) per the honest-nuance section.
