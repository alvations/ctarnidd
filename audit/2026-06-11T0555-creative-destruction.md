## Method

News-driven: Adobe's Q2 earnings landed June 11 amid the "AI eats creative software" narrative. Original
market-research piece, not investment advice. Forensic thesis: Adobe — a dominant software franchise — now
trades at a ~13x value-stock multiple because generative AI threatens its moat from below (Canva) and above
(OpenAI/Anthropic/Runway/Figma); Firefly is a moat-or-margin-leak bet (margins already eroding); it is either
a bargain or a melting ice cube, and the first domino testing whether AI strengthens or destroys software
incumbents. Anthropic's "Claude Design" named neutrally as one AI threat (same even-handed treatment as the
Anthropic IPO piece). Verified below.
