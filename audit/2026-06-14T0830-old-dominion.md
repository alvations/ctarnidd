## Method
Forensic thesis: ODFL is the best-run LTL carrier but a CYCLICAL in a freight recession (Q1 2026 rev -2.9% to
$1.33B, EPS $1.14 -4.2%, tonnage -7.7%, shipments -7.9%, OR +80bps to 76.2%) — yet the stock is +63% over six
months at ~45x earnings. Frames: cyclical-priced-as-secular (peak multiple on a trough); earnings-falling-while-
stock-rising (recovery front-run); pricing-discipline-can't-replace-volume (rev/shipment ex-fuel +4.7% vs tonnage
-7.7%); software-multiple-on-a-capital-intensive-trucker (category error); industry overcapacity post-Yellow;
bottom-of-cycle asymmetry (trough earnings + peak multiple offset). Honest-nuance: genuinely best-in-class (lowest
OR, best service), debt-free, counter-cyclical share gains, enormous operating leverage on recovery, has grown into
rich troughs before; Q2 guide (EPS $1.32) signals an expected rebound.
