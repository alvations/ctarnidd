# Audit — Tractor Supply's growth is rented from new stores while its old ones lose customers

Article: `2026-06-18T1000-tractor-supply.md`
Subject: Tractor Supply Company (NASDAQ: TSCO), specialty retail / rural lifestyle
Report under examination: Q1 fiscal 2026, reported April 21, 2026.

## Method

Forensic thesis: bought-growth-masks-organic-stall, plus cyclical-priced-as-secular. Tractor Supply's Q1 FY2026 net sales rose 3.6% to $3.59B, but comparable-store sales rose only 0.5% — and that gain was entirely a pricing/mix artifact (comparable average ticket +1.6%, comparable transactions -1.0%). The ~3-point gap between total and comparable sales is new-store contribution, i.e. rented growth. Meanwhile diluted EPS fell to $0.31 from $0.34 and net income fell to $164.5M from $179.4M — negative operating leverage driven by SG&A deleverage (~+6.1%, 29.7% of sales, ~70bps deleverage) while gross margin held flat at 36.2%. The article argues the "secular needs-based compounder" framing is undercut by negative traffic, big-ticket/mix-dependent comps, a pet-category drag (>100bps), softer new-customer acquisition reliant on new stores, and a reaffirmed full-year guide (comps 1-3%, EPS $2.13-$2.23) that requires a back-half acceleration the front half did not foreshadow — all against a full-ish ~15x trailing multiple with re-rate risk.

## Fact-check

CONFIRMED figures (multiple primary/secondary sources, incl. SEC 8-K and company IR):
- Net sales Q1 FY2026: $3.59B, up 3.6% (vs $3.47B prior year). CONFIRMED.
- Comparable-store sales: +0.5% (vs -0.9% prior-year quarter). CONFIRMED.
- Comparable average ticket: +1.6%; comparable transaction count: -1.0%. CONFIRMED.
- Retail price inflation ~150bps contribution to ticket; category mix from big-ticket. CONFIRMED.
- Diluted EPS: $0.31 (vs $0.34 prior year); missed ~$0.34-$0.35 consensus. CONFIRMED.
- Net income: $164.5M (vs $179.4M prior year). CONFIRMED.
- Gross margin: 36.2%, flat YoY. CONFIRMED.
- SG&A: ~$1.07B, up ~6.1%, 29.7% of sales, ~70bps deleverage. CONFIRMED.
- Operating margin: ~6.5% in quarter (vs ~7.2% prior-year quarter). CONFIRMED (per Simply Wall St / transcript).
- New stores opened: 40 namesake stores; one Petsense closed; new-store productivity 65-70%. CONFIRMED.
- Pet category: >100bps drag on comparable sales. CONFIRMED (earnings-call commentary).
- FY2026 guidance reaffirmed: net sales growth 4-6%, comps 1-3%, operating margin 9.3-9.6%, net income $1.11-$1.17B, diluted EPS $2.13-$2.23. CONFIRMED.
- Store base >2,300 namesake stores; long-term runway toward ~3,000. CONFIRMED (company framing).
- Trailing P/E ~15x (TTM cited at 14.6-15.6x across sources), below 10-yr median low-20s and below specialty-retail peer average. CONFIRMED as a range.
