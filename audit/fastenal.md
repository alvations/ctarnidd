# Audit — Fastenal grows 12% into a soft factory floor — at 35x, the math leaves no room to miss

Article: `2026-06-18T1430-fastenal.md` · Ticker: FAST · Sector: distribution

## Method

Forensic thesis: Fastenal is a cyclical, manufacturing-PMI-linked industrial distributor being valued at a secular-grower multiple (~35x forward earnings, ~89% premium to the ~18.5x industry median). The Q1 2026 print (reported April 13, 2026) was operationally strong — daily sales +12.4%, EPS +13.6% — yet the stock fell, because gross margin slipped 50bps to 44.6% as tariff-driven costs outran pricing. The forensic moves: (1) cyclical-priced-as-secular — revenue is umbilically tied to a mean-reverting PMI; (2) quality-of-earnings — EPS growth came from operating leverage + buybacks, not improving unit economics (gross margin fell); (3) bought/share-harvest growth decelerating toward the cycle; (4) denominator illusion in "daily sales"; (5) priced-for-perfection asymmetry — a de-rate from 35x to 25x is ~30% downside before any earnings miss; (6) capex tell — FMI is capital-heavy hardware, not asset-light software; (7) moat-as-tariff-funnel — the physical, China-sourced model channels duties straight into gross margin. Mandatory honest-nuance section concedes the genuine operating quality, moat durability, and cycle-upside optionality.

## Fact-check

CONFIRMED (primary + corroborating sources, Q1 2026 reported April 13, 2026, period ended March 31, 2026):
- Net sales: $2,201.7M, up 12.4% YoY (from $1,959.4M) — CONFIRMED (SEC 8-K, StockTitan, TradingView)
- Average daily sales: ~$34.9M vs ~$31.1M prior — CONFIRMED
- Diluted EPS: $0.30, up 13.6% YoY (from $0.26); met consensus — CONFIRMED
- Net income: $339.8M, up ~13.8% YoY — CONFIRMED (TradingView, StockTitan)
- Operating income: $447.6M = 20.3% of sales (op margin +20bps) — CONFIRMED
- Gross margin: 44.6%, down ~50bps from 45.1% prior year; ~40bps below internal Q1 target — CONFIRMED
- Realized pricing ~3.5% YoY; price/cost unfavorable ~50bps; tariff/transport/rebate drivers — CONFIRMED (earnings call / Investing.com transcript, StockStory)
- Operating cash flow $378.4M = ~111% of net income; ~$296M returned to shareholders — CONFIRMED
- Digital Footprint $1.37B = 61.5% of sales (vs $1.21B prior); FMI $1.0B = 44.9% (FASTStock $279.8M, FASTBin/FASTVend $721.6M) — CONFIRMED
- Onsite locations ($50k+/mo) up 16.3% YoY; ~250 new contract signings goal for 2026 — CONFIRMED
- FY2026 capex guide ~$320M = ~3.5% of net sales (high end of historical range) — CONFIRMED
- Forward P/E ~35x (GuruFocus ~34.9x, mid-May 2026); ~89% above industry median ~18.5x; historical 30-35x band — CONFIRMED (GuruFocus, peer-comparison sources)
- U.S. manufacturing PMI ~52.6 avg in Q1 2026; ISM Manufacturing PMI 54 in May 2026 (strongest since 2022); management's "3 months >50 → demand 3-4 months out" framing — CONFIRMED (TradingEconomics, S&P Global, ISM, earnings call)

Corrections made during drafting: an early query referenced a 44.9% gross margin; the verified figure is 44.6% (the 44.9% is FMI's share of sales, not gross margin). Article uses 44.6% throughout.
