# Audit — Carrier's data-center cooling story masks a 1% organic decline and a 44x multiple

Article: `2026-06-18T1630-carrier.md` · Ticker: CARR · Sector: industrials

## Method

Forensic thesis: Carrier Global has been re-rated to a secular-growth multiple (trailing P/E ~44 vs ~18 ten-year median) on a data-center-cooling / electrification narrative — 500% YoY data-center order growth, a backlog covering the full $1.5B 2026 data-center sales target. The forensic moves test whether a fast-growing sliver (~7% of revenue) justifies pricing the whole enterprise as a compounder, when the same Q1 2026 quarter delivered organic revenue DOWN ~1%, GAAP operating margin collapsing to 4.8% from 12.1%, GAAP EPS of $0.28 vs adjusted $0.57, net income falling to $238M from $412M, free cash flow of negative $15M, and CSA Residential sales down ~12%. Frames deployed: denominator illusion (500% of a small base); bought-growth-masks-organic-stall (Viessmann $14.2B buy + $10B+ divestitures); adjusted-vs-GAAP quality-of-earnings; cash-flow reality; cyclical-priced-as-secular (housing/China tether); demonstration-vs-deployment (backlog ≠ margin); customer concentration (hyperscalers); policy/subsidy dependence (European heat pumps). Mandatory honest-nuance section concedes the order strength is real, the installed-base/services annuity is underappreciated, and the residential weakness is partly destocking that can reverse.

- GAAP diluted EPS (continuing ops): $0.28; Adjusted diluted EPS: $0.57 (down 12% YoY; GAAP down ~40%) — CONFIRMED.

- GAAP operating margin: 4.8% vs 12.1% prior year (adjusted op margin 11.1% vs 16.3%) — CONFIRMED.
- Adjusted EBITDA: $866 million, 16.2% margin — CONFIRMED.
- Operating cash flow: $79 million; Free cash flow: NEGATIVE $15 million (vs $420M prior year) — CONFIRMED.
- Data-center orders up 500% YoY; backlog covers full-year $1.5B target; DC revenue ~$1B 2025 → ~$1.5B 2026 guide; prior Q4 NA DC orders up 400% — CONFIRMED.

- FY2025 sales ~$21.7B; 2026 guide rounds to slightly above $22B — CONFIRMED.
