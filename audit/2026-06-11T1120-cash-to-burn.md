## Method

Original market-research piece, not investment advice. Forensic thesis: the US EV-startup trade (Rivian, Lucid)
is a bet on companies that don't fund themselves — Lucid loses ~$3.50 operating per $1 revenue and lives on
Saudi PIF tranches; Rivian burns >$1B/quarter and leans on a Volkswagen software JV — while the category's
actual winner, BYD, already overtook Tesla in BEVs, is profitable, and sells at half the price, kept out of the
US only by a tariff wall that is itself an admission the startups can't compete on cost. Honest-nuance section
states the R2 / Gravity paths under which the bull case holds.

## Fact-check / verification (pre-fact-check `<commit>` → published; title "Cash to Burn" held)

Verified (two passes; nothing overstated):
- **Lucid Q1 2026**: revenue **$282.5M** (+20%); **net loss $1.02B**; **operating loss $989.4M**; delivered
  **3,093**; produced **5,500** (+149%). Op-loss/revenue ≈ 3.5× → "more than three dollars lost per dollar of
  revenue" holds. Liquidity ~$3.2B (~$4.7B pro forma). April raise ~$1.05B (PIF $550M conv pref + $300M common
  + $200M Uber).
- **Rivian Q1 2026**: revenue **$1,381M**; net loss **$416M**; adj EBITDA **$(472)M**; FCF **$(1,075)M**; cash+ST
  $4.83B / liquidity $5.39B; produced 10,236 / delivered 10,365; FY26 guide 62–67k; adj EBITDA guide $(1.8)–
  $(2.1)B, capex ~$2B. VW: $1.0B strategic capital, $282M JV revenue, $506M deconsolidation gain; R2 saleable
  production began (Normal, IL).
- **BYD**: overtook Tesla 2025 (~2.25M BEV vs ~1.63M, ~600k lead); Q3 profit −~33%; cut prices up to 30% (May);
  EU registrations +272% in a stretch, out-registered Tesla in EU; targeting ≤1.6M sales outside China;
  factories Thailand/Uzbekistan/Brazil (>300k/yr). Dolphin ~€35,500; Dolphin Surf ~€22,990; Model 3 ~€41,000.

Framing calls: tariff-wall and incentive-rollback sections are analysis/context (no single-source numeric claim
beyond what's verified); "demonstration vs deployment" is the series' recurring frame applied to EVs, not a new
factual assertion. No corrections required.
