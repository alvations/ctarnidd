story. Original market-research piece, not investment advice. Forensic thesis: Zuckerberg has suspended
budget discipline ("whatever it takes") to chase imminent superintelligence — but the depreciation bill
compounds into a mountain, the bet is funded by one mature ad engine now showing cracks, it's a systemic node

shareholders. Verified below.
