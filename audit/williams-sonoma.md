# Audit — Williams-Sonoma trades at 25 times earnings on a housing market that won't move

## Method

Forensic thesis: cyclical-priced-as-secular. Williams-Sonoma (WSM) posted a genuinely strong Q1 fiscal 2026 (reported May 21, 2026) — 4.8% comparable brand revenue growth, 44.0% gross margin, 16.2% operating margin, $1.93 diluted EPS, $373M returned to shareholders. The operating model is best-in-class. But the stock trades near $227, ~25x trailing earnings — a software-like multiple on the most housing-levered, big-ticket, discretionary cash flow in retail, into a housing market frozen near 4.1M existing-home sales (vs ~5M norm) for three years.

Forensic moves used: (1) denominator illusion — 4.8% comp is share-gain inside a ~2% market, not demand; (2) bought/concentrated-growth-masks-stall — West Elm +8.5% masks Pottery Barn +1.0%; (3) quality-of-earnings — margin contracting (gross -30bps, op -60bps YoY) against front-loaded tariffs; (4) buyback flattering EPS; (5) cyclical-priced-as-secular valuation; (6) asymmetry — bull needs 3 things right, bear needs 1 wrong; (7) B2B and base-comp timing traps. Mandatory honest-nuance section concedes best-in-class margins, share gains, fortress balance sheet, and that the bear thesis is about valuation/timing, not business quality or solvency.

## Fact-check

CONFIRMED (Q1 FY2026, reported May 21, 2026; 8-K / Business Wire / StockTitan / IR PDF):
- Total net revenue: $1.81 billion ($1,805,456 thousand). CONFIRMED.
- Comparable brand revenue: +4.8% overall. CONFIRMED.
- Brand comps: West Elm +8.5%, Williams Sonoma +5.0%, Pottery Barn Kids & Teen +4.5%, Pottery Barn +1.0%. CONFIRMED. B2B +13.7%. CONFIRMED.
- Gross margin: 44.0% (down ~30 bps YoY; ~100 bps merchandise-margin decline from tariffs/fuel, offset ~50 bps supply chain + ~20 bps occupancy). CONFIRMED.
- Operating margin: 16.2% (down ~60 bps YoY). CONFIRMED.
- Diluted EPS: $1.93 (up ~4.3% YoY). CONFIRMED. Net income ~$231M. CONFIRMED.
- Capital return: $288M buybacks + $85M dividends = $373M. CONFIRMED.
- FY2026 guidance: comp +2% to +6% (midpoint 4%); net revenue +2.7% to +6.7%; operating margin 17.5%–18.1% (midpoint 17.8%). Reaffirmed. CONFIRMED.
- Tariff commentary: front-loaded H1, peak Q2; planning assumes elevated oil, no tariff refunds, Section 232/301/122 rates continue. CONFIRMED.
- Stock ~$227, ~25x trailing earnings, trailing EPS ~$9, dividend $0.76/qtr, yield ~1.2%. CONFIRMED (as of June 19, 2026 market data; multiple stated as "roughly 25x trailing" — defensible range, trailing P/E quoted 20.7–24.8 across sources).
- Housing: existing-home sales ~4.1M annualized vs ~5M norm; lock-in suppressing ~870,000 sales in 2026; furniture/bedding spend forecast ~+1.9% 2026; big-ticket home purchases stalled, rotation to décor/essentials. CONFIRMED via housing/furniture-market coverage.

Corrections made during drafting: none material; an early query mistakenly probed "39%" gross margin — the verified figure is 44.0% and the article uses 44.0% throughout.
