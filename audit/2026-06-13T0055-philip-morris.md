## Method

Original market-research piece, not investment advice. Forensic thesis: PM re-rated from a cheap "sin stock" to a
premium growth stock on its smoke-free transformation (IQOS + ZYN now 43% of net revenue, adj EPS +16% to $1.96),
but the premium prices a clean, perpetual smoke-free story onto a business still 57% combustible cigarettes, whose
growth engines face tightening regulation, with jagged smoke-free shipment figures this quarter. Frames:
still-half-cigarettes + the mix-shift tailwind is finite; regulatory-arbitrage (pouches/heated tobacco enjoyed a
permissive tax/regulatory position regulators are now circling — FDA, flavors, youth, taxes); shipment NOISE (ZYN
shipments −23.5% but framed CHARITABLY and correctly as inventory normalization, NOT demand collapse); US/FDA
growth dependence + competition (BAT Velo, Altria on!); nicotine-company-by-any-name (ESG/social-license fragility);
premium-compression asymmetry. Honest-nuance: superbly executed transformation, IQOS #1 nicotine brand in many
markets, real harm-reduction, huge cash/dividend, ZYN consumer offtake +10% (the dip is inventory, not demand).
