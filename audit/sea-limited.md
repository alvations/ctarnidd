# Audit — Sea Limited (SE): revenue jumped 47% but Shopee's profit fell while volume surged 30%

## Method

Forensic thesis: Sea Limited's Q1 2026 (reported May 11, 2026) was a "record" headline quarter — consolidated revenue ~$7.1B (+47%), first-ever >$1B total adjusted EBITDA, stock up double digits premarket — but the most important profit line, Shopee segment adjusted EBITDA, fell year over year ($264.4M -> $223.2M, about -16%) even as Shopee GMV grew ~30% to a record $37.3B. The piece argues this is the swing back to growth-over-profit (logistics subsidies, VIP membership, user-acquisition incentives back on), with several forensic frames:

- bought-growth-masks-organic-stall (incentive-funded GMV vs. falling segment EBITDA)
- denominator illusion (EBITDA per dollar of GMV fell ~0.92% -> ~0.60%)
- cyclical-priced-as-secular (Garena's "best quarter since 2021" is a recovery off a trough on a concentrated title slate)
- quality-of-earnings (revenue +47% vs net income +7%; reliance on "adjusted" add-backs while building a depreciating logistics network)
- credit-risk denominator effect (Monee loan book +71% to $9.9B suppresses the visible 1.1% NPL ratio because young loans have not seasoned)
- competition (TikTok Shop in SEA, MercadoLibre in Brazil/LatAm forcing incentive spend up)
- FX translation effect on USD-reported emerging-market revenue
- priced-for-perfection asymmetry; mandatory honest-nuance concession section.

## Fact-check

CONFIRMED (Sea Limited Q1 2026 results, reported May 11, 2026; sources: businesswire/StockTitan 6-K, Investing.com, marketchameleon, Seeking Alpha):

- Total GAAP revenue: ~$7.1B (+46.6% YoY; Q1 2025 ~$4.8B) — CONFIRMED
- Net income: $438.2M (vs $410.8M Q1 2025, +6.7%) — CONFIRMED
- Total adjusted EBITDA: $1.034B (vs $946.5M Q1 2025), first ever >$1B — CONFIRMED
- Shopee GMV: $37.3B (+30.2%; Q1 2025 $28.6B) — CONFIRMED
- Shopee gross orders: +29% YoY — CONFIRMED
- Shopee segment adjusted EBITDA: Q1 2026 $223.2M vs Q1 2025 $264.4M, about -15.6% — CONFIRMED
- Garena bookings: $931.4M (+20.1%); Garena adjusted EBITDA $574M (+25%); revenue $697M (+41%); "best quarter since 2021"; QAU 666.5M; paying ratio 10.9% — CONFIRMED
- Monee/SeaMoney loan book: $9.9B consumer+SME outstanding (+71.3%); 38M+ active credit users (+35%); 90-day NPL 1.1% stable — CONFIRMED
- Monee/SeaMoney revenue +58%, adjusted EBITDA +14% (~$275M) — CONFIRMED
- 2026 guidance: ~25% Shopee GMV growth; full-year adjusted EBITDA "no lower than" 2025 in absolute dollars — CONFIRMED

CORRECTION MADE DURING FACT-CHECK: An initial secondary-source search reported Shopee Q1 2025 segment adjusted EBITDA as ~$464M, implying a ~52% YoY collapse. Verification against the official Q1 2025 results and the StockTitan 6-K showed the correct Q1 2025 figure is $264.4M, so the YoY decline is ~16% (about a third on a percent-of-GMV basis). The article was corrected throughout (headline, dek, body, kicker) to state ~16% / "fell while volume surged," removing all "cut in half" / "52%" / "$464M" language. Garena single-title claim softened to "concentrated-title" to reflect the record Arena of Valor contribution.
