# Datadog clears $1 billion a quarter while its GAAP margin sits at 1%

## Method

Forensic thesis: Datadog is a genuinely excellent observability platform priced as though its excellence is permanent. The article walks the GAAP-vs-non-GAAP bridge (1% GAAP operating margin vs 22% non-GAAP, driven almost entirely by ~$197M quarterly stock-based comp ≈ 19.6% of revenue), the priced-for-perfection multiple (~95x forward earnings, ~19-20x sales at ~$79B market cap), the cyclical-priced-as-secular risk in usage-based billing (revenue rides customer consumption and is exposed to cost-optimization), customer/cohort concentration in volatile AI-native customers, the trailing nature of net retention (low-120s NRR at a cycle top), the dilution-treadmill quality-of-earnings problem (SBC ≈ size of non-GAAP operating income), and a moat-vs-loophole frame (hyperscaler-native and open-source alternatives). A mandatory honest-nuance section concedes platform stickiness, broad AI adoption (~20% of customers / ~80% of ARR using AI integrations), GAAP profitability, and strong free cash flow ($289M).

## Fact-check

CONFIRMED (Q1 2026, reported May 7, 2026; primary = company IR / 8-K / StockTitan / Investing.com transcript):
- Revenue $1,006M, +32% YoY — first quarter above $1B. CONFIRMED.
- GAAP operating income $7M; GAAP operating margin 1%. CONFIRMED (company release / StockTitan).
- Non-GAAP operating income $223M; non-GAAP operating margin 22%. CONFIRMED.
- Stock-based compensation $197M in the quarter (~19.6% of revenue). CONFIRMED (earnings coverage).
- GAAP net income per diluted share $0.15. CONFIRMED (earnings transcript coverage).
- Non-GAAP EPS $0.60; non-GAAP net income ~$218M. CONFIRMED (Yahoo/Zacks).
- ARR > $4B; ~4,550 $100k+ ARR customers vs ~3,770 a year ago. CONFIRMED.
- RPO $3.48B, +51% YoY. CONFIRMED.
- Free cash flow $289M. CONFIRMED.
- TTM net revenue retention low-120s, up from ~120; gross retention mid-to-high 90s. CONFIRMED (call commentary).
- ~20% of customers using AI integrations, representing ~80% of ARR. CONFIRMED (call commentary).
- FY2026 guidance: revenue $4.3B-$4.34B (25%-27%); non-GAAP operating income $940M-$980M (22%-23%); non-GAAP EPS $2.36-$2.44. CONFIRMED.
- Q2 2026 guidance: revenue $1.07B-$1.08B (29%-31%). CONFIRMED.
- Market cap ~$79.29B (as of June 18, 2026); forward P/E ~95. CONFIRMED (stockanalysis/Yahoo). P/S ~19-20x derived from ~$79B cap and ~$4B run-rate revenue (labeled as derived in article).

Corrections: none required. Contested/derived figures (P/S multiple, AI-native cohort contribution) are presented as ranges or as "on the order of" rather than precise assertions.
