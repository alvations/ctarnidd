# Audit — Deere trades near record highs on trough earnings as its core farm engine shrinks 14%

Article: `2026-06-18T1530-deere.md` · ticker DE · sector industrials

## Method

The forensic thesis is **cyclical-priced-as-secular**, reinforced by **bought/mix-growth-masks-core-stall** and a **quality-of-earnings** flag. Deere's fiscal-Q2-2026 headline (revenue +5%, net income -2%) reads as resilience, but the consolidated number is a blend that buries a 14% sales decline and a 22.0%→15.7% operating-margin collapse in Production & Precision Agriculture — the flagship large-ag franchise that underpins the company's premium "precision-ag / tech" valuation. Growth came from lower-margin Construction & Forestry (+29%) and Small Ag & Turf (+16%). FY2026 net income is guided to $4.5–5.0B, roughly half of the FY2023 peak ($10.166B), yet the stock trades within ~12% of its all-time high at >32x trailing / ~30x forward earnings — a peak multiple on trough earnings. A $272M tariff (IEEPA) refund lifted equipment margins ~2.5pts to 16.9% (non-recurring). Management calls FY2026 the cycle bottom with recovery in 2027; the article argues the stock already prices that recovery, creating a priced-for-perfection asymmetry.

## Fact-check

CONFIRMED figures (primary + reputable secondary sources):
- FY26 Q2 (quarter ended ~late April 2026): net income **$1.773B**, EPS **$6.55**; net sales & revenues **+5% to $13.369B**; net income **-2%** YoY. (Deere IR / PR Newswire / StockTitan)
- Production & Precision Agriculture: sales **-14% to $4.503B**; operating margin **22.0% → 15.7%**. (StockTitan / farm-equipment.com)
- Small Ag & Turf: sales **+16%**, operating margin **20.6%**. Construction & Forestry: sales **+29%**, operating margin **14.8%**. (StockTitan)
- FY26 guidance: net income **$4.5B–$5.0B**; equipment-operations cash flow **$4.5B–$5.5B**. Large-ag industry volumes guided **-15% to -20%** (US/Canada); South America tractor/combine **~-15%**; production agriculture **-5% to -10%**; construction sales growth target raised to **~20%**; small ag **~+15%**. (BigGo / Yahoo Finance / mlq.ai)
- Tariff refund **$272M** (IEEPA-related) lifted equipment margins **~2.5pts to 16.9%**. (Yahoo Finance / mlq.ai)
- Stock ~**$585.29** close (June 16, 2026); ATH close **$660.56** (Feb 20, 2026); 52-wk high **$674.19**; market cap **~$159.74B**; trailing P/E **~32.6**; forward P/E **~29.77**. (Macrotrends / Investing.com / CNBC)
- John Deere Financial Q2 net income up double-digits (≈18%). (Equipment Finance News)

CORRECTION applied during fact-check: the peak earnings year is **fiscal 2023 ($10.166B)**, NOT fiscal 2024. Fiscal 2024 net income was **$7.1B**. The draft initially referenced a "2024 peak ~$10.2B"; corrected throughout to FY2023 peak of $10.166B, with FY2024 noted as the $7.1B step-down. The "roughly half of peak" framing remains accurate-to-conservative ($4.5–5.0B vs $10.166B is under half). (PR Newswire FY2023/FY2024 releases)

No invented numbers. Contested/derived items (12% from ATH; "less than half of peak") are arithmetic on confirmed figures.
