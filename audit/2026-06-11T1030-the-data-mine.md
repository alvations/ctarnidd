## Method

Original market-research piece, not investment advice. Forensic thesis: Reddit monetizes its users' unpaid
words as AI training data (Google ~$60M/yr, portfolio could reach ~$550M) and has been re-rated as an AI
winner — but the same AI it sells to (Google AI Overviews / zero-click) is choking the search traffic that
produces new posts, so the mine is selling the ore that empties it; the recurring-vs-one-time licensing
question undercuts the annuity thesis either way; and the whose-data-is-it user-revolt risk threatens the
free-labor engine. WSB-favorite, volatile. Verified below.

No corrections; all figures matched on verification. "Selling the ore that empties the mine," recurring-vs-
