# Audit — Waste Management trades at 31x earnings while 2026 growth rides almost entirely on price, not volume

Article: `2026-06-18T1730-waste-management.md`
Ticker: WM (NYSE) · Sector: business-services · Date: 2026-06-18

## Method

Forensic thesis: WM is a genuinely excellent, irreplaceable toll-road waste business that the market has mispriced as a growth stock. The premium (~31x trailing P/E, >50% above the waste-industry median) is being paid for revenue growth that is, by the company's own 2026 guidance, almost entirely price (core price 5.4–5.8%) rather than volume (0.2–0.6%). The thesis stacks several forensic frames against verified facts: (1) growth-is-price/organic-volume-stall; (2) revenue-miss-papered-over-by-EPS-beat (quality of earnings); (3) bought-growth-on-borrowed-balance-sheet (Stericycle, leverage to ~3.6x); (4) denominator illusion in "FCF nearly doubled"; (5) policy/subsidy-dependent RNG optionality already capitalized; (6) cyclical-priced-as-secular; (7) priced-for-perfection multiple; (8) thinned dividend cushion; (9) execution risk in a CFO transition overlapping integration + capex. A mandatory honest-nuance section concedes the real moat (irreplaceable landfill network), genuine pricing discipline, credible sustainability upside, sound Stericycle strategic logic, and recession durability.

- Q1 2026 operating revenue: $6.227 billion; missed consensus ~$6.287 billion. CONFIRMED (GuruFocus, Investing.com, Quiver, StockTitan 10-Q).
- Q1 2026 adjusted diluted EPS: $1.81; beat estimate (~$1.70 per initial search; one source cites $1.79). Article states beat past $1.70 estimate — CONFIRMED as a beat; the precise consensus is given conservatively.
- Adjusted operating EBITDA up ~5.9% with margin expansion; cash from operations ~$1.5B, up ~24% YoY; FCF nearly doubled. CONFIRMED.
- FY2026 guidance: revenue $26.43–26.63B (sources: $26.425–26.625B); adjusted operating EBITDA $8.15–8.25B. CONFIRMED.
- 2026 collection & disposal guidance: core price 5.4–5.8%, yield 3.2–3.6%, volume 0.2–0.6%. CONFIRMED (WM Q4/FY2025 release; Waste Dive; Seeking Alpha).
- Stericycle acquisition: $7.2B including ~$1.4B Stericycle net debt; >$125M run-rate synergies; announced June 2024. CONFIRMED (WM IR news release).
- Leverage: ~3.4x immediately post-close, target 2.75–3.0x ~18 months out; reported to have moved to ~3.6x. CONFIRMED.
- Healthcare Solutions ~3% 2026 revenue growth, mainly price; ERP/integration friction. CONFIRMED (Waste Dive, Simply Wall St).
- Sustainability: 6 RNG projects + 4 recycling projects due 2026; >$400M tuck-in M&A in 2025. CONFIRMED (Waste Dive).
- Valuation: trailing P/E ~31.2–31.4 (52.5% above industry median ~20.59); forward P/E ~26.7; EV/EBITDA ~14.3; dividend yield ~1.54%. CONFIRMED (GuruFocus, stockanalysis.com, fullratio).
- RSG outperformed WM over trailing 12 months (+22% vs +6%); 10-yr CAGR RSG 17.25% vs WM 15.82%. CONFIRMED.
