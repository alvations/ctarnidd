# Audit — FEMSA's 97% profit surge is mostly an accounting mirage — strip it and earnings fell 36%

Article: `2026-06-21T0930-femsa.md` · Ticker: FMX (FEMSA ADR, NYSE) · Sector: staples

## Method

Quality-of-earnings forensic teardown of FEMSA's Q1 2026 results (reported April 30, 2026). The thesis: the headline 97.3% surge in net consolidated income (Ps. 17,639M) is dominated by a one-time, non-recurring accounting gain from the BradyPLUS–Imperial Dade transaction; strip it and underlying net income FELL 36.4% to Ps. 5,688M, dragged by higher net financing expenses and the absence of prior-year discontinued-operations income (a denominator illusion). Secondary frames: (1) two-FEMSAs split — a brilliant Oxxo Mexico engine (op income +20.9%) bolted to a flat/declining Coca-Cola FEMSA bottler (op income -2.3%); (2) comparable-vs-reported metric steering (op income +12.1% comparable vs +5.5% reported); (3) cyclical-priced-as-secular — Oxxo's consumer is levered to peso FX and US remittance flows; (4) un-modelable US–Mexico tariff/political risk; (5) FEMSA Forward divestitures returned capital but shrank the recurring earnings base. Honest-nuance section concedes Oxxo execution, the Spin fintech flywheel, governance follow-through, and genuine staples defensiveness.

## Fact-check

CONFIRMED (FEMSA IR press release + StockTitan 6-K coverage, Q1 2026 reported April 30, 2026):
- Net consolidated income Ps. 17,639M, +97.3% vs 1Q25 — CONFIRMED (FEMSA IR).
- Net income excluding BradyPLUS–Imperial Dade one-time gain Ps. 5,688M, -36.4% — CONFIRMED (FEMSA IR), attributed to higher net financing expenses + absence of discontinued-operations income.
- Total revenue growth +6.1% reported / +8.5% comparable — CONFIRMED (FEMSA IR).
- Operating income +5.5% reported / +12.1% comparable — CONFIRMED (StockTitan).
- Gross profit +6.6% reported, gross margin ~40.5% — CONFIRMED (StockTitan/search).
- Oxxo Mexico: revenue +8.3%, op income +20.9%, SSS +6.0%, gross profit +11.5% — CONFIRMED (StockTitan).
- Coca-Cola FEMSA: revenue +1.1% to ~Ps. 70,925M (~US$3.9B), op income -2.3%, gross profit +4.5% — CONFIRMED (StockTitan/search).
- Americas & Mobility: revenue +12.9% reported, op income +34.0% reported — CONFIRMED (StockTitan).
- Spin by Oxxo 11.0M active users (+22.3%); Spin Premia 28.4M (+12.8%); digital tender 50.6% vs 42.5% — CONFIRMED (StockTitan).
- Stock +5.56% on announcement day — CONFIRMED (StockTitan).
- Net majority income Ps. 4.34 per FEMSA Unit (+167.9%); US$2.41 per ADS — CONFIRMED (FEMSA IR).
- FEMSA Forward / Heineken divestiture (~14.76% stake at end-2022, sold down via share sales + exchangeable bonds), plastics-solutions sale to AMMI — CONFIRMED (FEMSA/SEC/Bloomberg Línea coverage).

CORRECTION applied: an early draft stated absolute Q1 revenue of "Ps. 149.3 billion." The official FEMSA IR release confirms the +6.1%/+8.5% growth but does not state that absolute total; a separate source cited Ps. 207,784M on a different consolidation basis. To avoid asserting a contested absolute figure, the article was edited to state revenue growth percentages only ("total revenue up 6.1% year over year"), which are fully confirmed.
