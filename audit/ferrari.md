# Audit — Ferrari shipped fewer cars again — and trades at 34x betting it never needs to ship more

Article: `2026-06-21T1730-ferrari.md` · Ticker: RACE · Sector: luxury

## Method

Forensic thesis: Ferrari (RACE) is priced as a perpetual-pricing-power compounder (~34x trailing earnings) rather than the cyclical luxury automaker it is. Q1 2026 showed shipments DOWN 4.4% (3,436 vs 3,593) while revenue still rose to ~€1.85B — growth that came entirely from price/mix/personalization, not units. The piece argues this is a denominator illusion: a deliberately capped volume model means all growth must come from a price lever that has a ceiling. The multiple embeds flawless pricing power, flawless EV brand-transfer (Luce/Elettrica, deliveries Q4 2026, 2030 EV target already cut), flawless macro (US wealth-effect-driven country mix), and an order book into 2027 — multiplying low-probability conditions. Forensic frames used: denominator illusion, cyclical-priced-as-secular, quality-of-earnings (flat net profit, adjusted/constant-currency framing), demonstration-vs-deployment (Luce unsold), moat-vs-mix, customer/geographic concentration, priced-for-perfection asymmetry, EV mix-dilution.

## Fact-check

CONFIRMED figures (Ferrari Q1 2026 results, reported May 5, 2026; SEC Form 6-K; company press release):
- Q1 2026 net revenues: ~€1.85 billion (€1,848M); +3.2% reported, +6.0% constant currency. CONFIRMED.
- Q1 2026 net profit: €413 million, flat vs prior year. CONFIRMED.
- Q1 2026 EBITDA: €722 million; margin 39.1%. CONFIRMED.
- Q1 2026 EBIT (operating profit): €548 million; margin 29.7%. CONFIRMED.
- Q1 2026 shipments: 3,436 units, down 4.4% from 3,593 in Q1 2025. CONFIRMED.
- 2026 guidance confirmed: net revenues ~€7.50B; adjusted EBITDA ≥€2.93B (margin ≥39.0%); adjusted EBIT ≥€2.22B (margin ≥29.5%); adjusted diluted EPS ≥€9.45; industrial free cash flow ≥€1.50B. CONFIRMED.
- Order book extends into / toward end of 2027. CONFIRMED.
- Mix drivers: F80, Special Series, personalization, Americas country mix; absence of Daytona SP3. CONFIRMED.
- PE ratio ~33.9 trailing / ~31.7 forward (May 15, 2026); auto industry ~16x, luxury peer avg ~26x, "fair" ~20x. CONFIRMED (financecharts / simplywall.st).
- EV "Luce" (development name Elettrica): unveiled May 26, 2026; price from €550,000 (~$640,000); >1,000 hp, 4 motors, 122-kWh battery, top speed >310 km/h, >2.2 tons, first 5-seater/4-door; LoveFrom (Jony Ive) involvement; deliveries begin Q4 2026; stock fell on reveal. CONFIRMED.
- 2030 EV sales target cut at October 2025 Capital Markets Day. CONFIRMED.

Corrections made during drafting: none required; the EV's public name is "Luce" (Elettrica = internal/development name) — article states both correctly. No figure asserted beyond what primary/secondary sources confirm. Price-multiple labelled as ~34x (trailing) consistent with 33.9x source.
