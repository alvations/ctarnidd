## Method

Original market-research piece, not investment advice. Forensic thesis: AppLovin is a genuinely, hugely
profitable company whose rich multiple is a measure of trust in AXON — a black-box ad engine it won't open.
Credible short-sellers allege the edge depends on ToS-violating data practices (company DENIES; shorts lost on
price), the contested e-commerce expansion IS the growth that justifies the multiple, the business runs on
platform "land" Apple/Meta control, and an SEC probe is now examining the exact (data-collection) question.
Balance is deliberate: allegations labeled as allegations, profits acknowledged as real, shorts' price failure
stated. Verified below.
