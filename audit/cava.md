# Audit — CAVA trades at 175x earnings while its own guidance halves same-store growth

## Method

Forensic thesis: CAVA Group is a genuinely excellent fast-casual operator priced as a flawless one. The activist-short framing is NOT fraud (there is none) but valuation asymmetry — "priced for perfection." The load-bearing forensic moves:

1. **Deceleration in the company's own forecast** — Q1 FY2026 same-restaurant sales of 9.7% vs. full-year guidance in a mid-single-digit range (variously reported 4.5%–7%), implying comps roughly halve over the year. This is management's own number, not a bear projection.
2. **Margin guided lower** — Q1 restaurant-level margin 25.1% vs. full-year guide 23.7%–24.3%; the market pays for operating leverage / margin expansion while the company forecasts compression.
3. **Quality-of-earnings** — GAAP net income FELL YoY to $23.6M from $25.7M (EPS $0.20 vs $0.22) despite 32.2% revenue growth and 37.6% adjusted-EBITDA growth; the case is argued on adjusted EBITDA (excludes SBC) while the 175x P/E is computed on GAAP earnings the bulls dismiss as tax-distorted.
4. **Denominator illusion** — $3.0M AUV is strong but stable, not rising, while unit count grows ~20%/yr; growth by multiplication of a stable unit, not improvement of the unit.
5. **Cyclical-priced-as-secular**, **Chipotle comparison cuts the other way** (CMG trades at a lower multiple on 8x the footprint and 30 years of proof), **single-lever concentration risk**, **priced-for-perfection asymmetry**.

Mandatory honest-nuance section concedes: traffic-led comp (6.8%), Chipotle-class 25.1% margin, pristine balance sheet (~$403M cash, zero debt), self-funded growth, new units at 100%+ productivity, real category, raised (not cut) guidance.

## Fact-check

CONFIRMED figures (Q1 FY2026, reported May 19, 2026):
- Revenue $434.4M, +32.2% YoY — CONFIRMED (multiple sources, businesswire/8-K).
- Same-restaurant sales +9.7%, traffic +6.8% — CONFIRMED.
- Restaurant-level profit $108.9M, margin 25.1% (vs 25.1% prior year) — CONFIRMED.
- Adjusted EBITDA $61.7M, +37.6% — CONFIRMED.
- GAAP net income $23.6M (down from $25.7M); diluted EPS $0.20 (down from $0.22; beat $0.18 est) — CONFIRMED.
- 20 net new restaurants; 459 total units; ~20.2% YoY unit growth — CONFIRMED.
- AUV ~$3.0M system-wide; new units at/above 100% productivity — CONFIRMED.
- Cash & investments ~$403M, zero debt; operating cash flow $64.1M (vs $38.6M) — CONFIRMED.
- Full-year 2026 guidance: 75–77 net new openings; restaurant-level margin 23.7%–24.3%; adjusted EBITDA $181M–$191M — CONFIRMED.
- Valuation (mid-June 2026): ~$80/share, ~$11B market cap, P/E ~175 — CONFIRMED (stockanalysis/public.com/CNBC quote data).
- FY2025 revenue $1.18B — CONFIRMED.

CORRECTION applied: Same-restaurant-sales guidance was reported inconsistently across sources — one primary 8-K-derived source said 4.5%–6.5%, another said 5%–7%. Softened all article references to a defensible "mid-single-digit range (roughly 4.5%–7%)" to avoid asserting a single contested figure. Thesis (comps roughly halving from 9.7%) holds under both readings.

Nothing in the article asserts fraud; all forensic claims are valuation/interpretation, clearly labeled as such.
