# Audit — SoundHound AI trades at 16 times sales on $44M a quarter and a widening GAAP loss

## Method

Forensic thesis: SoundHound AI (SOUN) is the purest voice-AI hype trade on the tape — a company with tens of millions of dollars of quarterly revenue capitalized near $2.7–3.0 billion (~16x trailing sales, forward P/E reported above 200), where headline growth is substantially acquisition-fueled (SYNQ3, Allset, Amelia, pending LivePerson), GAAP gross margin is only ~31 percent (not a software margin), GAAP net losses run ~$25M/quarter, and the diluted share count compounds faster than the business (denominator illusion). The angle's original "customer concentration" premise is now WEAKER — concentration has materially improved (no customer >10% in the most recent disclosure, vs. >70% historically) — so the article concedes this fully in the mandatory bull section and pivots the bear case to bought-growth, quality-of-earnings (GAAP vs. non-GAAP, "one-time" vendor costs), earn-out/contingent-consideration noise, dilution, and priced-for-perfection asymmetry.

## Fact-check

CONFIRMED (Q1 2026, reported May 7, 2026; SEC Form 8-K / company IR / StockTitan / GlobeNewswire):
- Revenue $44.2M, +52% YoY — CONFIRMED.
- GAAP gross margin 31% (31.1%); non-GAAP gross margin 50% (49.7%) — CONFIRMED.
- GAAP net loss $(25.0)M — CONFIRMED; included a ~$39M non-operating, non-cash charge tied to acquisition-related contingent liabilities / stock-price movement — CONFIRMED.
- R&D $26.2M (+6%); S&M $19.2M (+60%) — CONFIRMED.
- Automotive/IoT organic growth ~88% excluding acquisitions — CONFIRMED.
- FY2025 revenue $168.9M, up ~99% — CONFIRMED.
- FY2026 guidance $225–260M — CONFIRMED.
- 2027 framing $350–400M and "$500M revenue opportunity," contingent on LivePerson close — CONFIRMED as management projection (labeled as projection in article).
- LivePerson: all-stock deal, equity value ~$43–45M, ~22% premium, EV ~$250M after debt; expected to close 2H 2026 — CONFIRMED.
- Goodwill ~$101M, intangibles net ~$160M (mid-2025) — CONFIRMED.
- Acquisitions: SYNQ3 (~$25M, early 2024), Allset (assets), Amelia (~$80M, Aug 2024, >$45M recurring software rev) — CONFIRMED.
- Diluted share count: ~338M (2024 avg) → ~414M (early 2025) → ~426M (2026); ~55% dilution in 2024, ~20% trailing-twelve-month increase — CONFIRMED via multiple aggregators.
- Market cap ~$2.7–3.0B; ~16x trailing sales; forward P/E reported above 200; price ~$6.9–7.4 (June 2026) — CONFIRMED (ranges used in article, day-dependent).
- Customer concentration: historically >70% single customer / >90% automotive; most recent disclosure no customer >10% — CONFIRMED.

CORRECTION applied during fact-check:
- Cash balance: initial sources cited ~$248M; verification search cited ~$216M end-of-Q1. SOFTENED in article to "low-to-mid $200 millions" / "low-to-mid $200 million range" to stay within a defensible range rather than assert a single contested figure.
