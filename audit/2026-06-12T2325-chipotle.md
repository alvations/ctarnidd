# Audit — Chipotle (2026-06-12T2325-chipotle.md)

Title: "Chipotle Is Priced for Growth While Its Same-Store Sales Have Stopped Growing." Method + fact-check; kept
out of the article. See `../SOURCES.md` and `../sources/2026-06-12T2325-chipotle/`. Batch 10, article 3 of 10
(target 2,900+ words; actual ~2,900). Source: news.

## Method

Original market-research piece, not investment advice. Forensic thesis: Chipotle trades at a technology-like
premium that depends on same-store compounding, but Q1 2026 comps were just +0.5% (barely breaking a 3-quarter
losing streak; transactions +60bps, check −10bps), adjusted EPS FELL 17% to $0.24, restaurant-level margin
compressed 250bps to 23.7% (beef/freight), and FY comps are guided ~flat (pricing only 1-2%). Revenue +7.4% is now
driven mostly by new-unit construction, not same-store magic. Frames: comp-stall (the metric that earns the
multiple); earnings-down-on-revenue-up (anti-operating-leverage); unit-growth-not-comp-growth (capital-intensive +
saturating; cyclical/mature priced as secular); tapped-out price lever + stretched value-seeking customer;
multiple-compression asymmetry; new fast-casual competition (CAVA et al.) where there was open field; digital
(38.6%) + throughput plateau. Honest-nuance: best-in-class operator, industry-leading 23.7% margin even after the
dip, long unit runway + international optionality, Q1 RETURN to positive transactions, margin dip partly cyclical,
has reaccelerated comps before.

## Fact-check / verification (pre-fact-check commit → published; all confirmed)

CONFIRMED (Chipotle Q1 2026 8-K / IR release / 10-Q / CNBC / Motley Fool transcript, reported Apr 29 2026):
- Revenue **$3.1B (+7.4%)** ✓
- **Comparable restaurant sales +0.5%** (transactions +60bps, check −10bps) ✓ — broke a 3-quarter comp-decline streak
- **Adjusted diluted EPS $0.24, −17% YoY** ✓ (GAAP EPS ~$0.23, an EPS miss vs ~$0.25 est)
- **Restaurant-level margin 23.7%** (adj for legal settlements), **−250bps YoY** (beef + freight) ✓
- **Digital sales $1.2B = 38.6% of total** ✓
- FY2026 guide: **same-store sales ~flat**; Q2 ~+1%; **pricing 1-2%** ✓
- Unit growth: 49 US/Canada openings in Q1; first Europe site (record opening-day sales); Mexico + South Korea
  markets planned 2026 (supports "unit runway long / international barely begun")

### Note (no correction)
- No exact forward P/E asserted in the article (sources didn't confirm one); valuation kept qualitative
  ("technology-like multiple," "far above mature peers like McDonald's"). Chipotle's premium-to-restaurant-peers
  multiple is well established and defensible as a qualitative claim. CMG did a 50:1 split (2024); article cites no
  share price, so split-adjustment is moot.

### Editorial care
- Conceded the Q1 transaction turn + long runway + cyclical margin dip explicitly (honest-nuance). Competition
  framed as "credible alternatives" without naming a specific comp-growth number for rivals (avoids an unverified
  stat). "Earnings fell" is GAAP-and-adjusted true. Price-lever / customer-stress framed as the company's own 1-2%
  pricing guide implies, not as a prediction. No price target; no buy/sell language.

No corrections to load-bearing facts.
