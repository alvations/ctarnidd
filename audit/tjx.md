# TJX beats, raises, and still leaves no margin for error at 34 times earnings

Article: `2026-06-20T1300-tjx.md` · Subject: The TJX Companies, Inc. (NYSE: TJX) · Sector: retail (off-price)

## Method

The assigned angle (maturing trade-down tailwind, wage/freight margin compression, normalizing traffic, priced-for-perfection) was VERIFIED against the most recent quarter and PARTIALLY PIVOTED. Q1 FY27 (reported May 20, 2026) was a clear beat with margin EXPANSION (+1.7pp), not compression, and traffic-led comps of 6% — so the "margins press" and "comps already normalized" premises were false for the current print and were corrected.

The retained, accurate forensic thesis: TJX is an elite operator, but the stock trades at a mid-30s forward P/E (~31–35x), more than double the cyclical-retail median (~15x), on a pretax margin sitting near its all-time ceiling, while the company's OWN raised guidance forecasts deceleration (6% Q1 comp → 3%–4% full year). The bull case requires management to keep beating its sandbagged guidance indefinitely; meeting the plan would be a negative catalyst. Forensic frames deployed: cyclical-priced-as-secular; priced-for-perfection asymmetry; denominator illusion (buyback-driven EPS); margin-near-ceiling; moat-vs-loophole (conceded as moat); guidance-implied deceleration.
