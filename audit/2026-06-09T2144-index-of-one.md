## Corrections (pre-fact-check `149c0ea` → published)

- **Nvidia $5T timing:** the draft said "in 2026 became the first… $5 trillion." Nvidia first reached
  $5T **in 2025** (it is ~$5.05T in June 2026). Removed "in 2026" to avoid mis-dating the milestone.
- All other figures verified as drafted: Mag 7 ≈34% of S&P (12% in 2015); top 10 ≈40% (22% in 2020);
  Nvidia ≈22% of Mag 7; 2022 S&P −20.4% vs Mag 7 −41.3%; ~5 largest = greatest US concentration in ~half
  a century; same names ≈20% of MSCI World; Goldman's 2026 Mag-7-underperform call; equal-weight rotation.
- Title "Index of One" set at draft and retained.
