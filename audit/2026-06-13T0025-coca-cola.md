## Method

Original market-research piece, not investment advice. Forensic thesis: KO's growth is structurally PRICE-led, not
volume. Q1 2026 unit case volume (real demand) +3%; price/mix +2%; the headline organic +10% = concentrate sales
+8% (inflated by 6 extra selling days + shipment timing) + price/mix +2%, vs a FY guide of just +4-5% — and KO
itself said that excluding the extra days + timing, organic was in line with the FY guide. Frames: all-price /
denominator (volume is the compounding engine, price is a finite ratchet); timing-flattered headline vs sober FY
guide (read the guidance, not the print); affordability ceiling (K-shaped consumer → premium/value split = a tacit
admission of the limit); GLP-1 structural VOLUME threat (KO's "shift not abandon" rests on its own research; slow
erosion is what a defensive premium least absorbs); defensive bond-proxy premium vulnerable in a higher-rate world.
Honest-nuance: elite brand portfolio + distribution moat, genuine pricing power, 60+ yr dividend-growth record,
real diversification (Fairlife/water/Coke Zero) that genuinely positions it for a GLP-1 world, Q1 beat + raised EPS
guide, thoughtful K-shaped strategy.

## Fact-check / verification (pre-fact-check commit → sharpened → post commit)

CONFIRMED (KO Q1 2026 press release / 8-K / earnings call / CNBC / Investing.com, reported Apr 28 2026):
- **Organic revenue +10%** = **concentrate sales +8%** (lifted by **6 extra days** + shipment timing) **+ price/mix
  +2%** ✓
- **Unit case volume +3%** (led by China, US, India) ✓
- KO: **excluding the 6 extra days + shipment timing, organic growth was in line with the FY guide** ✓ — directly
  supports the "timing-flattered headline" thesis
- **FY2026 guide: organic revenue +4-5%**; comparable currency-neutral EPS +6-7% (comparable EPS +8-9% incl tax
  effects) ✓
- Stock popped ~6% on the beat ✓
- GLP-1: KO says users shift to low/no-cal (Coke Zero, Diet Coke, smartwater, Fairlife, sports drinks) + smaller
  packs; launched single-serve mini cans (Jan 2026) ✓; K-shaped premium/value strategy ✓
