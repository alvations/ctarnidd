# Audit — Guardant Health races to $1.3B in revenue while losses keep widening past $110M a quarter

Article: `2026-06-17T1130-guardant.md` · Ticker: GH · Sector: diagnostics

## Method

Forensic thesis: Guardant Health (GH) is priced for a colorectal-screening franchise it has not yet earned. Q1 2026 produced the fastest revenue growth of the company's public life (48% to $302M; Shield screening revenue +600% to $41.6M) yet the GAAP net loss WIDENED to $112.1M (from $95.2M a year earlier), full-year 2025 free-cash-flow burn was $233M, and growth is being financed by ~$700M+ of fresh equity and convertible debt rather than profits. The piece uses these forensic frames: profitability-is-theoretical (loss grew as revenue scaled), denominator illusion ($1B trailing revenue vs quarter-billion burn), quality-of-earnings (stock-comp add-back in non-GAAP), demonstration-vs-deployment (Shield's 83.1% CRC sensitivity but 13.2% advanced-adenoma sensitivity), priced-for-a-TAM vs judged-by-a-P&L, competition (Exact Sciences Cologuard Plus, Abbott), policy/reimbursement dependence ($1,495 Medicare rate), and the dilution flywheel / priced-for-perfection asymmetry. A mandatory honest-nuance section concedes the broad-based growth, the genuine unmet-need that Shield serves, improving burn, and ample runway.

## Fact-check

CONFIRMED figures (primary + reputable secondary sources):

- Q1 2026 total revenue: $302M, +48% YoY (vs ~$203.5M prior-year quarter). CONFIRMED — Investing.com, Yahoo Finance transcript, SEC 8-K exhibit 99.1.
- Oncology revenue: $205M, +36%, ~86,000 tests, +47% volume. CONFIRMED — Barchart/Yahoo/Globe & Mail transcripts.
- Biopharma & Data revenue: $53M, +17%. CONFIRMED.
- Screening revenue: $41.6M, up from $5.7M prior-year period (>600% growth); Shield volume ~44,000 tests vs ~9,000 prior year. CONFIRMED — StockTitan 10-Q, SEC 8-K.
- Net loss Q1 2026: $112.1M; loss per share $0.85; prior-year quarter loss $95.2M (loss WIDENED). CONFIRMED — minichart/StockTitan/Investing.com.
- Free cash flow burn Q1 2026: $71M (YoY rise attributed to higher annual bonus payout; ex-bonus, burn down $12M). CONFIRMED.
- Full-year 2025 FCF burn: $233M, improved from $275M in 2024. CONFIRMED — Q4 2025 release / Kavout.
- Full-year 2026 guidance: $1.30B–$1.32B revenue (≈32%–34% growth); screening revenue $162M–$174M from 210,000–225,000 Shield tests. CONFIRMED — IR press release, Investing.com.
- Capital raised late 2025: $327.3M public offering + $402.5M convertible senior notes (>$700M total). CONFIRMED — Q4 2025 release / Kavout.
- Shield Medicare reimbursement: $1,495 per test (NOT the $920 in the suggested angle; corrected). CONFIRMED — Kavout/IR. Screening gross margin improved to 56% from 18% on Shield cost-per-test reduction to $420 and higher ASP after Medicare fee update. CONFIRMED — Seeking Alpha/Barchart.
- Shield clinical profile (Eclipse study): CRC sensitivity 83.1%; advanced-adenoma sensitivity 13.2%; specificity ~90%; Stage I–III sensitivity 87.5%. CONFIRMED — NEJM/PMC, FDA SSED, MedTech Dive.
- Exact Sciences Cologuard Plus: CRC sensitivity ~93.9%, advanced-adenoma (APL) sensitivity ~43.4%. CONFIRMED — Exact/peer-reviewed summaries.
- Abbott developing a blood-based colorectal screening test. CONFIRMED — industry reporting.

Corrections made vs the suggested angle: the Medicare rate is $1,495 (not $920); no "ADT loophole" framing used because it could not be sourced. All competitive-sensitivity comparisons are stated as study-reported figures, not adjudicated head-to-head superiority claims.

Report date: Q1 2026 results reported May 7–8, 2026.
