  The **$4B+ is the all-insider 2024 total.** Rewrote with accurate per-person figures: **Karp ~$2B (2024)**,
  **Thiel ~$1.5B**, **Cohen ~$310M**, **Sankar (CTO) >$420M** — a fuller, correct roster.
- **Combined-figure line** changed from "two people… five billion off the table" to "founders and officers…
  more than four billion in a single year."
- Verified as drafted: **P/S ~67–70**; **P/E ~154** (>150); Q1 revenue +85%; FY guide $7.66B (~71%); gross
  margin 84%, GAAP-profitable; insiders net −$2.7B (12 mo); down ~a third from $207 high, ~20% in 2026;
  +2,110% since start of 2023.
