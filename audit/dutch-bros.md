# Audit — Dutch Bros trades at 50 times earnings while two-thirds of its growth is freshly poured concrete

## Method

Forensic thesis: Dutch Bros (BROS) is a drive-thru specialty-coffee operator priced like a secular software compounder — roughly 50x forward earnings and ~35x forward EV/EBITDA on an ~$11B market cap. The article decomposes the headline 31% Q1 2026 revenue growth to show it is overwhelmingly new-unit ("bought") growth — only ~8.3 points are organic same-shop sales, of which only ~1.5 points are price — and argues the multiple embeds an assumption of flawless unit expansion (≥185 openings/year toward ~2,029 shops by 2029) into a commodity-exposed, cyclical, traffic-led business. Frames used: bought-growth-masks-organic-stall, denominator illusion (comp survivorship), commodity price-taker, adjusted-vs-GAAP quality-of-earnings, priced-for-perfection asymmetry, cyclical-priced-as-secular, moat-vs-loophole. Mandatory honest-nuance section concedes traffic-led growth, runway, execution, and healthy 28.3% shop contribution margin.

## Fact-check

CONFIRMED (Q1 2026, reported May 6, 2026; multiple independent sources incl. SEC 8-K ex-99.1, Motley Fool transcript, Investing.com, StockTitan, MarketBeat):
- Total revenue: $464.4M, +31% YoY (one source rounds to +30.8%). CONFIRMED.
- Company-operated shop revenue: $429M, +31% (+$103M). CONFIRMED.
- Systemwide same-shop sales: +8.3% (best since Q1 2024); systemwide transactions +5.1%. CONFIRMED.
- Company-operated same-shop sales: +10.6%; company-operated transactions: +6.9%. CONFIRMED.
- Price contribution to comp: ~1.5 points; rest transaction-led. CONFIRMED (transcript).
- GAAP net income: $23.7M vs $22.5M prior year. CONFIRMED.
- Adjusted EBITDA: $79.4M, +26.2% (vs $62.9M). CONFIRMED.
- Adjusted EPS: $0.16 (beat $0.15). CONFIRMED.
- Company-operated shop contribution margin: ~28.3%. CONFIRMED (transcript).
- New shops opened Q1: 41 (incl. 7 Clutch conversions). CONFIRMED.
- Total shops at quarter end: 1,177. CONFIRMED.
- FY2026 guidance: revenue $2.05–$2.08B (25–27% growth); adj EBITDA $370–$380M; ≥185 system openings; SSS +4–6%. CONFIRMED.
- Coffee cost commentary: ~$2.80–$3.00 range; ~60 bps total COGS pressure and ~30 bps net adj EBITDA margin pressure in 2026; beverage/food/packaging +120 bps YoY in Q1. CONFIRMED (transcript).
- Valuation: forward P/E ~52.5; EV/EBITDA ~35.7; share price ~$53–$59 around report; ~187.7M shares; ~$11.1B market cap; stock fell >6% after print. CONFIRMED (stockanalysis/valueinvesting/ainvest; price labeled approximate range).
- Long-term framing: path toward ~2,029 shops by 2029. CONFIRMED (multiple).

Corrections made during drafting: none material; valuation multiples and share price labeled as approximate/"roughly" given live-quote drift. Texas ~20% SSS labeled "reportedly."
