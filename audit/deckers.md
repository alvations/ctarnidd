# Audit — Deckers' Hoka cools to 15% as a two-brand empire prices in perfection

## Method

Forensic thesis: Deckers (DECK) is structurally a two-brand company — Hoka and Ugg together are ~97% of revenue — and its growth engine has visibly decelerated. Hoka grew from mid-to-high-20s historically to 16% for FY2026 and 15% in its largest-ever quarter, with management guiding low-double-digit growth and a high-single-digit June quarter. Ugg is a seasonal, fashion-cyclical boot brand guided to mid-single digits. The article applies several forensic frames: bought-growth-masks-organic-stall (growth-engine downshift), cyclical-priced-as-secular (Ugg), customer/channel concentration (US wholesale softness, international crutch), quality-of-earnings (gross margin guided down on freight/inputs/$120M tariffs), denominator illusion (records vs rates), priced-for-perfection asymmetry (15x forward, framework reset to high-single-digit CAGR), and competitive squeeze (On above, Nike below). The mandatory honest-nuance section concedes elite margins, a fortress balance sheet, a $5B buyback, a cheap-ish 15x multiple post de-rating, and genuine brand credibility.

## Fact-check

CONFIRMED figures (sources: Deckers IR press release detail/656; SEC 8-K FY2026; Yahoo Finance / just-style; TIKR; Investing.com; StockTitan; Simply Wall St):

- FY2026 net sales: $5.47B, +10% (reported ~9.8%) — CONFIRMED.
- Hoka FY2026 net sales: $2.59B, +16% (reported ~15.9%) — CONFIRMED.
- Ugg FY2026 net sales: $2.74B, +8% (reported ~8.2%) — CONFIRMED.
- Hoka + Ugg ≈ 97% of $5.47B total — CONFIRMED by arithmetic ($5.33B / $5.47B = 97.4%).
- Q4 FY2026 revenue: $1.12B, +10%, beat ~$1.09B est. — CONFIRMED.
- Hoka Q4 revenue: $671M, +15%, largest quarter in brand history — CONFIRMED.
- FY2026 diluted EPS: $7.02 vs $6.33 prior, +11% — CONFIRMED.
- Operating margin: above 23% — CONFIRMED (exceeded 21.5% earlier guide).
- FY2027 guidance: revenue $5.86–5.91B; diluted EPS $7.30–7.45 — CONFIRMED.
- FY2027 gross margin guide: ~56.5%, down YoY on freight (Middle East shipping disruption), input/material costs — CONFIRMED.
- Tariffs: $120M paid; guidance assumes 10% rate full year, no refund, refund pursuit ongoing — CONFIRMED.
- Through-2030 framework: high-single-digit revenue CAGR; Hoka low double digits; Ugg mid-single digits; EPS low double digits — CONFIRMED.
- June quarter: Hoka guided to high-single-digit growth, attributed to one-time wholesale timing shifts — CONFIRMED.
- Stock ~$110.17 as of June 16, 2026; ~14.9x (article rounds to "roughly 15x") forward P/E; Raymond James downgrade Strong Buy → Outperform on valuation — CONFIRMED.
- $5B buyback authorization — CONFIRMED (StockTitan 8-K, BigGo).

Corrections applied: none required; article rounds ~9.8%→10%, ~15.9%→16%, ~8.2%→8%, ~14.9x→15x, all within defensible ranges and labeled approximate ("roughly," "~"). Competitive claims about On (faster growth off smaller base) and Nike (running turnaround) framed as qualitative pressure, not specific unverified figures.
