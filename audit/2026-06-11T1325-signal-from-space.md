## Method

Original market-research piece, not investment advice. Forensic thesis: AST SpaceMobile's demonstration is
genuinely miraculous (broadband from orbit to an unmodified smartphone, 98.9 Mbps; AT&T/Verizon partners;
antenna 35–40× Starlink's per-satellite DTC array) — but the ~$26–36B valuation on ~$15M quarterly revenue and a
$191M quarterly loss prices the prophecy, not the business. The deployment is a dilutive, multi-year marathon
(shares +437%/5y, +33%/1y; the $3.5B cash was all raised, including a Feb convertible-notes offering, not
earned), each satellite an unfolding knife-edge, against a far better-capitalized SpaceX/Starlink chasing the
same prize. Recurring series frames invoked: infinite TAM, demonstration-vs-deployment, dilution-funded capital
sink, the space-SPAC base rate. Honest-nuance sections concede the technology is the best of its breed and a
real (future-conditional) path to the valuation exists.

## Fact-check / verification (pre-fact-check commit → published; title "Signal From Space" held)

CONFIRMED (Motley Fool transcript / StockTitan / SEC 8-K):
- Q1 2026 revenue **$14.7M**; net loss to common **$191.0M**; total opex **$164.1M**; cash **~$3.5B** (3/31/26),
  inclusive of a **Feb 2026 convertible-notes** offering (2.25% 10-yr, strike $116.30).
- FY2026 guidance **reaffirmed ($150–200M)**, explicitly **contingent** on Block 2 BlueBird launch/deployment,
  milestones, gateway sales, and commercial-service activation.
- 2025 revenue ~$70.92M; loss ~−$341.94M.
- Tech: **98.9 Mbps** to an unmodified phone; BlueBird 6 largest LEO commercial array; targeting ~**45**
  BlueBird sats in 2026; partners **AT&T/Verizon**; antenna **35–40×** Starlink's DTC; Starlink partners
  **T-Mobile**.

DATE-SAFETY: A Falcon 9 BlueBird 8/9/10 launch was reported for **June 17, 2026** — *after* this article's
2026-06-11 dateline. The article deliberately does **not** state that launch as completed; it uses "targeting
~45 ... with further launches planned." No false past-tense claim. ✅

SECONDARY (traced to search-01, not reconfirmed in the verify query; kept as sourced): dilution **+437%/5y,
