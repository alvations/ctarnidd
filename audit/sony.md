# Audit — Sony's record ¥1.45 trillion profit hides a console past its peak and a ¥208 billion write-off year

Article: `2026-06-20T1230-sony.md` · Ticker: SONY (Sony Group ADR) · Sector: media

## Method

Forensic thesis: Sony Group is priced as a content/IP compounder (durable, secular, asset-light) while its record fiscal 2025 (ended March 31, 2026) was built on cyclical engines turning the wrong way — a PS5 console past its shipment peak (2.8M units in the closing quarter vs 9.5M the prior quarter; 93.7M lifetime), an image-sensor business levered to the smartphone replacement cycle, and a financial-services arm spun out in October 2025 that removed the steadiest, least-cyclical counterweight. The "record" ¥1.45 trillion operating income absorbed ~¥208B of impairments (Bungie ¥120.1B, EV-exit ¥44.9B, Pixomondo ¥27.1B), making it a quality-of-earnings question. FY2026 guidance itself shows revenue DOWN ~1.4% with profit UP ~10.5% — a margin-expansion story dressed as growth. Frames used: cyclical-priced-as-secular; quality-of-earnings (adjusted-vs-GAAP); denominator illusion (spin-off reshaping); customer/partner concentration (sensors); commodity/tariff price-taker; priced-for-perfection asymmetry; moat-vs-harvest.
