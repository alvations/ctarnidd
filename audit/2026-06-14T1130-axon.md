## Method
Forensic thesis: AXON is a genuinely elite, near-flawless growth company (Q1 2026 rev +34% to $807.3M, 9th straight
quarter >30%, ARR +35% to $1.5B, NRR 125%, future bookings +44% to $14.3B, AI product revenue +700%, raised guide),
so the bear case is almost entirely VALUATION: ~57x forward earnings (below its own 80-100x historical range, but
high in absolute terms) prices perpetual perfection. Frames: priced-for-perfection asymmetry (no room for "very
good"); government-budget customer (cyclical/political/fiscal exposure beneath the recurring-revenue smoothness);
new-TAM (AI/counter-drone/enterprise/international) valued as already-won off a small base (infinite-TAM,
demonstration-vs-deployment); SBC/large founder-pay packages dilute + flatter adjusted earnings; near-monopoly
invites antitrust + customer pushback. Honest-nuance: near-monopoly moat + hardware-software-AI flywheel, 125% NRR,
$14.3B backlog, explosive AI, brilliant execution, ambitious 2028 targets ($6B rev), multiple below its own history.

### Editorial care
- This is the rare article where the business is conceded as near-flawless and the thesis is EXPLICITLY almost
  only price — stated up front and in honest-nuance. The 57x is framed honestly as below its own history (the bull
  point) but high in absolute terms. Government-budget cyclicality, new-TAM-off-small-base, SBC dilution, and
  antitrust/monopoly scrutiny are all real, documented risks, framed as underweighted-by-the-premium rather than
  imminent. AI +700% explicitly noted as "off a small base." No price target endorsed (analyst $708/80%-upside
  noted only here). No buy/sell. Not Apple-related.
